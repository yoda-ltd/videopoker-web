# Risk vs. Reward: Super Star Poker vs. Dream Card

## The Risk: 5-Coin Volume vs. 10-Coin Precision
- **Super Star Poker (The Volume Bet):** You typically bet 5 coins per line across multiple hands (3, 5, or 10-play). The risk is spread horizontally. If you lose one hand, you might win the others.
- **Dream Card (The Precision Bet):** You bet 10 coins per line. The risk is concentrated. You are paying double to ensure that the machine "Assists" you on approximately 50% of your hands.

## The Reward: Accumulated Multipliers vs. Hand Completion
- **Super Star Poker:** The reward comes from the "Menu" of choices. If you play the *Ultimate X* variant within Super Star, your rewards are **Accumulated Multipliers** (up to 12x) earned for future hands. The "Peak Reward" is a Royal Flush with a 12x multiplier.
- **Dream Card:** The reward is "Guaranteed Completion." The machine provides the mathematically optimal fifth card to your four best cards. The "Peak Reward" is seeing the machine "Dream" a Royal Flush or Four-of-a-Kind into existence before you even make a discard.

## Variance Normalization: The Bankroll Squeeze
- **Super Star Poker:** Has a "Smoothing" effect. By playing 10 hands at 5 coins each (50 coins total), you achieve **Variance Normalization** more quickly. Your bankroll moves in a relatively predictable wave.
- **Dream Card:** Has a "Jagged" effect. Even if you play only 5 hands, your cost is the same (50 coins). However, because you are betting 10 coins per hand, the "Dry Spells" (the 50% of hands where no Dream Card appears) are brutal. You lose money twice as fast when the feature is dormant.

## Side-Bet EV: The Professional's Edge
- **Super Star Poker:** The EV is tied to "Pure Strategy" and game selection. You "Shop" for the best pay table (like 9/6 Jacks or Better) and rely on your skill to achieve the 99.54% RTP.
- **Dream Card:** The EV is tied to the "Bonus Choice." By paying the 10th coin, you elevate the RTP to nearly 99.8%. The "Reward" for your 10-coin "Risk" is the lowest house edge on the casino floor.

- **Dream Card:** The feature triggers every 2 hands. The **Feature Hit Frequency** is incredibly high, providing constant "Dopamine Hits" as the machine "Corrects" your hands.

- **Choose Super Star Poker** if your goal is "Sustainability." It is a lower-risk, higher-volume platform that rewards standard professional strategy and careful game selection.
- **Choose Dream Card** if your goal is "The Big Win" and you have the bankroll to handle high-risk, 10-coin-per-line volatility. It rewards the aggressive player who wants the machine to "Co-Pilot" their session toward a high RTP.
