# Common Mistakes to Avoid in Bingo Poker

Bingo Poker is a fascinating hybrid that adds a bingo-style component to the standard video poker game. While the added layer of complexity is fun, it also creates many opportunities for player error that can tank your **Theoretical Return**.

### Ignoring the Bingo Card
The most obvious mistake in Bingo Poker is ignoring the bingo card altogether. The bingo component adds a significant percentage to the RTP. If you don't adjust your strategy to occasionally chase "bingo numbers," you are playing at a disadvantage. However, the opposite is also true: over-chasing bingo numbers at the expense of a high-paying poker hand is a disaster for **Hit Frequency Optimization**.

### Bankroll Mismanagement
Bingo Poker tends to have higher **Variance** than standard variants because the payouts are split between the poker hands and the bingo wins. For **Session Sustainability**, you need a bankroll that can handle "dry spells" in both games simultaneously. Without proper **Variance Buffering**, you might find yourself out of cash just as the bingo card is about to fill.

### "Is Max Bet Always Better for Bankroll Longevity?"
In Bingo Poker, the answer is usually yes. Often, the bingo jackpots or bonus payouts are only fully unlocked at the 5-coin level. If you are a beginner looking for **video poker tips for beginners**, our advice is to always check the help screen of a Bingo Poker machine to see which features are bet-dependent.

### Misunderstanding the RNG
Bingo Poker often "feels" different, leading some players to believe the **Pseudo-Randomness** is rigged to favor the bingo card or the poker hand at different times. In reality, a single RNG determines the outcome of both. The cards are random, and the bingo numbers are drawn from a randomized set. It is all math, not a "mood" of the machine.

### Strategy Confusion
Many players try to use a standard Jacks or Better strategy for Bingo Poker. This is a mistake. To learn **how to win video poker daily** on a Bingo Poker machine, you must use a strategy that specifically accounts for the added value of the bingo card.

Stay focused on both boards, manage your bankroll, and Bingo Poker can be a highly profitable and entertaining game.
