# Advanced Pick'em Poker Strategy for Pro Players: Binary EV Optimization

Pick'em Poker (also known as Pick A Pair) is a unique variant where the player is dealt two cards on the left, and then must choose between two cards on the right to complete their hand. For the professional, Pick'em Poker is the "Purest" game of **Binary EV Optimization**. Because there is only one "Decision" (Pick A or Pick B), achieving a **mathematically perfect video poker** performance is about "Zero-Error Execution." To succeed, one must master **Optimal Path Convergence** for the "Split-Deck" and utilize **Recursion Analysis** to evaluate the "Hidden Hands."

## Binary EV Optimization: The "One-Pick" Decision

In Pick'em Poker, your hand consists of 2 cards you *always* keep and 2 cards you *choose* between (one of which completes a 5-card hand).

1.  **The "Fixed-Base" Strategy**: In **advanced video poker strategy**, your "Base" (the two left-most cards) determines your "Optimal Path." If your base is "Ace-Ace," your "Pick" is always focused on the "4-of-a-Kind" or "Full House" path.
2.  **Binary EV Optimization**: Unlike standard games where you have 32 possible hold combinations, Pick'em Poker only has two. A professional uses **Recursion Analysis** to calculate the "Expected Value" of each of the two 3-card "Stacks" on the right. One stack will *always* have a higher EV, even if the "Visual" card is weaker.

## Recursion Analysis: The "Hidden" Card Value

In Pick'em Poker, you see the "Top Card" of two separate 3-card stacks. When you pick a stack, the two "Hidden Cards" underneath are revealed to complete your 5-card hand.

- **Optimal Path Convergence**: The "Top Card" is not the whole story. The **mathematically perfect video poker** player knows the "Probability Distribution" of the hidden cards. - **Edge Case Prioritization**: Pros use **Edge Case Prioritization** to decide between two "Marginal" stacks. If Stack A shows a "High Card" (King) and Stack B shows a "Straight Connector" (9), the pro knows exactly at what "Base Hand" configuration the 9 becomes the superior "Optimal Path."
