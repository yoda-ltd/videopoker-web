## The Multiplier Mechanic: Vertical Volatility

In Ultimate X, the payout for a winning hand is multiplied by a factor (e.g., 2x, 4x, 12x) that was earned on the previous hand.
- **The Impact on the Royal:** If you hit a Royal Flush on a line with a 12x multiplier, the payout is **48,000 coins**.

## Royal Flush Odds and the Independent Draw

In Ultimate X, as in any 52-card game, the odds of hitting a Royal Flush are approximately 1 in 40,000 per hand. 
- **The Smoothing Effect:** In a 10-play Ultimate X machine, you are running 10 **Independent Trials** per deal. The **Independent Draw Randomness** ensures that over time, your frequency of hitting a Royal Flush on one of the lines will match the theoretical probability.
- **Shared-Base Variance:** However, your total payout is heavily influenced by the **Shared Base Hand**. If you are dealt 4-to-a-Royal on the deal, you have 10 independent shots at a potentially multiplied jackpot.
