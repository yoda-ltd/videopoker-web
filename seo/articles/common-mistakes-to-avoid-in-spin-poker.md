# Common Mistakes to Avoid in Spin Poker

Spin Poker is a high-action video poker variant that combines the draw poker experience with a slot machine layout (9 or 20 paylines). While the multi-line action is thrilling, it also leads to several common player errors.

### Confusing "Spin" with "Slot"
The biggest mistake in Spin Poker is treating it like a slot machine. While it looks like a slot, the outcomes are determined by the cards you hold and draw. If you don't use a perfect video poker strategy, your **Theoretical Return** will plummet. You must use the same **Hit Frequency Optimization** as you would for a single-hand game, but applied across multiple lines.

### Bankroll Mismanagement
Because you are playing 9 or 20 lines at once, your cost per "spin" is much higher. A 5-cent machine with 20 lines and a 5-coin max bet costs $5.00 per hand. This dramatically increases the **Variance**. To maintain **Session Sustainability**, you need a much larger bankroll for **Variance Buffering**. If you aren't prepared for the cost, you will bust out in minutes.

### "Is Max Bet Always Better for Bankroll Longevity?"
In Spin Poker, the answer is yes. To get the Royal Flush bonus and the best RTP, you must bet 5 coins *per line*. If you are playing 20 lines, that's a 100-coin bet. For those looking for **video poker tips for beginners**, our advice is to play fewer lines at max bet rather than more lines at a single coin per line.

### Strategy Confusion
Many players get overwhelmed by the multiple lines and start making mistakes on their holds. Remember: you only make one set of "holds," and those holds are applied to all active lines. To learn **how to win video poker daily** on a Spin Poker machine, you must focus entirely on the "main" hand and ignore the noise of the other lines.

### Misunderstanding the RNG
Some players think that if they win on multiple lines, the machine is "hot" and will continue to pay out. This is a misunderstanding of **Pseudo-Randomness**. Each "spin" is an independent event. The RNG deals from a fresh 52-card deck for every hand, regardless of what happened on the previous one.

Spin Poker is a fantastic game for those who love high-velocity action. Avoid these mistakes, manage your bankroll, and you'll find it to be a highly rewarding experience.
