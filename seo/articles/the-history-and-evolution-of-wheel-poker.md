# The History and Evolution of Wheel Poker

Wheel Poker is a "Thematic Revolution" in the history of video poker. It represents the moment when the "Wheel of Fortune" phenomenon—the most popular slot mechanic in history—was successfully integrated into the strategic world of poker.

## The "Bonus Wheel" Concept

In the late 1990s and early 2000s, IGT's *Wheel of Fortune* was the undisputed king of the casino floor. Players loved the "Physicality" and "Drama" of the spinning wheel. Developers realized that video poker players wanted a piece of that "Bonus Wheel" action.

Wheel Poker was the solution. By betting a "Bonus Bet" (usually 6 to 10 coins), players activated the "Wheel" feature. Whenever a "Four of a Kind" (or in some versions, a "Full House") occurred, the machine would trigger a "Virtual" or "Physical" wheel spin for a massive bonus prize.

## Evolutionary Impact on the Market

1.  **The "Jackpot within a Jackpot" Phase:** This was the first major video poker game to offer a "Second Chance" for a massive win. Hitting Four of a Kind was already a great win; the "Wheel" made it a "Life-Changing" win.
2.  **Increased Coin-In:** Like "Ultimate X" and "Dream Card," Wheel Poker successfully increased the "Cost of Play." By making the "Wheel" the star of the show, players were happy to bet more per hand for the chance to "Spin the Wheel."
3.  **Visual and Audio Drama:** Wheel Poker introduced "Celebratory" sound effects and music that were more common in slot machines. The "Spin the Wheel!" call-out became a familiar sound on the casino floor.

## Essential Video Poker Terminology

When consulting the complete video poker glossary, these are the video poker terms every player should know for Wheel Poker:

*   **Bonus Wheel:** The randomized wheel that determines the extra payout for a qualifying hand.
*   **Variance:** This is a "High Variance" game. Much of the machine's "Expected Value" (EV) is tied up in the "Wheel" bonuses, meaning the "Base" game can feel "Tighter" than standard poker.
*   **Expected Value (EV):** To achieve a high EV in Wheel Poker, you must play with a "Wheel-Aware" strategy. This often means being even more aggressive in chasing "Four of a Kind," as that is the "Key" to the wheel.

## Strategy and "The Spin Focus"

The evolution of Wheel Poker created "Targeted Strategy." In standard poker, a Full House is a great way to "Stay in the Game." In some versions of Wheel Poker, a Full House doesn't trigger the wheel, but a Four of a Kind does. This forced players to evolve their strategy to prioritize "Quads" above all else.

## Legacy and Modern Availability

Wheel Poker paved the way for modern hits like "Wheel of Fortune Video Poker" and "Quick Quads Wheel Poker." It proved that video poker players enjoyed the "Spectacle" of the wheel just as much as slot players.

Today, Wheel Poker remains a "High-Visibility" attraction on every major casino floor. It is a reminder that in the modern casino, "Innovation is about Giving the Player a Show." It remains a legendary entry in the "Complete Video Poker Glossary."
