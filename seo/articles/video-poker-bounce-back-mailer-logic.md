# Video Poker "Bounce Back" Mailers: Decoding the Logic

In the casino industry, the "Holy Grail" of marketing is the **Bounce Back** mailer. For the video poker player, this is the "Rebate" that turns a break-even game into a profitable one. Understanding the **Mailer Logic** behind these offers is the key to mastering the **best video poker rewards strategy**.

## 1. What is a "Bounce Back" Mailer?

A bounce back is a personalized offer sent to you (via mail or email) after a visit to a casino. It typically includes:
*   **Free Slot Play:** $20 to $1,000+ per week.
*   **Complimentary Rooms:** 2 to 4 nights with no blackouts.
*   **Food Credit:** $25 to $500 "Dining Credit."

## 2. The Algorithm: ADT and Recentness

Casinos use a "Weighted Moving Average" to determine your mailer value. The two biggest factors are:

### A. ADT (Average Daily Theoretical)
The casino looks at your play history over the last 12-18 months, with a heavy weight on your most recent 3 trips. 
*   **The Math:** If your **ADT** is $200, the casino might be willing to "spend" $60 (30%) per day to get you back. That $60 is split between the cost of the room and your Free Play.

### B. "The Gap" (Recentness)
Casinos use a "Re-Activation" strategy. If you haven't visited in 4-6 months, your mailers will often *increase* in value. This is a "Lure" offer to see if they can win back your loyalty.

## 3. Professional Strategy: "The Tease"

Elite players often use "The Tease" to trigger higher mailers without a massive bankroll commitment.

1.  **The Seed Trip:** Visit a new casino property.
2.  **High-Intensity Play:** Play for 4 hours at a high denomination ($5 or $10) on a **Zero-Play Day** (meaning you aren't staying at the hotel).
3.  **The Result:** The system sees a "New Player" with a massive ADT. Because you aren't "costing" them a room yet, the **Mailer Logic** will likely send you a "Whale" offer (Suites + High Free Play) to entice you to stay.

## 4. Why Mailers Stop (and How to Restart Them)

If your mailers have dried up, you likely committed one of the "Three Sins of Comp Hustling":

*   **Sin 1: The Zero-Play Day.** You stayed at the hotel but didn't play. This crushed your ADT average.
*   **Sin 2: "Hit and Run."** You used your Free Play and left without putting in any "New Money" coin-in. The system records this as a "Negative Value" visit.
*   **Sin 3: "Over-Staying."** You stayed for 5 nights but only played for 2.

**The Fix:** You must "Flush the System." Visit the casino for a "Play Only" day (no hotel stay) and put in a high-volume session. This resets your ADT to a high level.

## 5. Integrating Mailers into the "Vegas Loop"

If you are following the **best video poker rewards strategy**, your mailers are your calendar.

*   **The Rotation:** You should have 4-5 different casino operators in your rotation (MGM, Caesars, Station, Boyd, Wynn). 
*   **The Chain:** Use the MGM mailer for Week 1, the Caesars mailer for Week 2, and the Station mailer for Week 3. By the time you get back to Week 1, the MGM system has a fresh offer waiting for you.

## 6. Digital vs. Physical Mailers

In 2026, **Digital Mailers** (via casino apps) are more common and more reactive.
*   **The Pro Tip:** Check your "Offers" tab every Friday. Digital offers can be "Dynamic," meaning they change based on hotel occupancy. If the hotel is empty, your $50 Free Play might suddenly jump to $100 for that specific weekend.

The "Bounce Back" mailer is the casino's attempt to buy your future business.
