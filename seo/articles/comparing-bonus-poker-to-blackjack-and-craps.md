# Comparing Bonus Poker to Blackjack and Craps

Bonus Poker is often the "entry point" for video poker players who want a bit more excitement than Jacks or Better. But how does it stack up against the titans of the casino floor: Blackjack and Craps?

### The RTP Comparison
- **Bonus Poker (8/5)**: Has a **Theoretical Return** of 99.17% with perfect strategy.
- **Blackjack**: Offers a 99.5% RTP (0.5% house edge) with perfect basic strategy.
- **Craps (Pass Line)**: Offers a 98.59% RTP (1.41% house edge).

While Blackjack has a slightly higher base RTP, Bonus Poker offers something Blackjack doesn't: the potential for a massive payout (the Royal Flush) on a small bet.

### Variance and Sustainability
Bonus Poker has higher **Variance** than both Blackjack and Craps because the pay table is "top-heavy" with bonus payouts for Four-of-a-Kind hands. For **Session Sustainability**, you need a larger bankroll for Bonus Poker than you would for a slow-paced game of Craps. Without proper **Variance Buffering**, the swings of Bonus Poker can be punishing.

### The Max Bet Question
"Is max bet always better for bankroll longevity?" In Blackjack and Craps, your bet size is irrelevant to the house edge. In Bonus Poker, the 5th coin is essential. If you aren't playing max bet, the RTP of an 8/5 machine drops significantly, making it a much worse bet than Craps.

### Skill and Strategy
Blackjack requires constant decision-making. Craps is mostly about avoiding the "sucker bets" on the layout. Bonus Poker requires a specific strategy that focuses on **Hit Frequency Optimization** for the bonus hands. For those looking for **video poker tips for beginners**, Bonus Poker is slightly harder to master than Blackjack but offers a more solitary and relaxed playing experience.

### RNG vs. Physical Odds
While Craps relies on physical dice and Blackjack on a physical or shuffled deck, Bonus Poker uses **Pseudo-Randomness** through an RNG. This ensures that every hand is fair and independent, just like the roll of the dice in a certified game of Craps.

If you want to know **how to win video poker daily**, understanding these comparisons is vital. Bonus Poker offers a great balance of risk and reward, provided you play with perfect strategy and a max bet.
