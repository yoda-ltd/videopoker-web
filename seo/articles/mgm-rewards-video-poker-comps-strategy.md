## Understanding the Mechanics of MGM Rewards

MGM's program is significantly different from Caesars. It is more tied to "points" and less to "theo," though both still matter for your **ADT**.

### 1. Slot Dollars and Tier Credits
*   **Slot Dollars:** These are your "Free Play" earned from video poker. You earn them at a rate of 1 Slot Dollar for every $10 coin-in on most video poker machines.
*   **Tier Credits (TC):** You earn TCs through play and spending. In 2026, the TC earn rate is consistent with $100 coin-in = 1 TC on most machines, making it more challenging to level up than other programs—unless you're playing high-limit.

### 2. The "Pearl" and "Gold" Sweet Spots
*   **Pearl:** Essential for the "free self-parking" benefit.
*   **Gold:** The most important tier for the serious video poker grinder. At Gold status, **Resort Fees are Waived**. In a city where resort fees have hit $50+ per night, this benefit alone is worth hundreds of dollars per trip.

## Professional Gambling Terminology at MGM

*   **Theo:** Your Theoretical Loss, which MGM uses to calculate "Back-End Comps" (discretionary comps given by a host at the end of your stay).
*   **Bounce Back:** The "Free Play" or "Resort Credit" offers you receive in the mail or via the MGM Rewards app about 2-4 weeks after your trip.
*   **Slot Club Strategy:** Always "check out" with a host before leaving. If your **ADT** was higher than your actual loss, the host can often "wipe" your remaining room charges (food, spa, etc.) against your Theo.
