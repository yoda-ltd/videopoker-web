# Comparing Royal Aces to Blackjack and Craps

Royal Aces is a high-excitement video poker variant that offers a significantly higher payout for a Royal Flush. How does this "top-heavy" game compare to the classic table games?

### RTP and the Royal Payout
- **Royal Aces**: The **Theoretical Return** can be very high, often exceeding 99% on well-configured machines.
- **Blackjack**: Offers a 99.5% RTP (0.5% house edge) with perfect basic strategy.
- **Craps (Pass Line)**: Offers a 98.59% RTP (1.41% house edge).

While Blackjack still holds a slight edge on base RTP, Royal Aces provides the chance for a massive payout (the Royal Flush) that Blackjack simply doesn't offer on a single bet.

### Variance and Sustainability
Royal Aces has much higher **Variance** than both Blackjack and Craps because a significant portion of its return is tied up in the Royal Flush payout. To achieve **Session Sustainability**, you need a much larger bankroll for **Variance Buffering** than you would for a slow-paced game of Blackjack. Without the extra bankroll, the long wait for a Royal Flush can be punishing.

### The Max Bet Factor
"Is max bet always better for bankroll longevity?" In Blackjack and Craps, your bet size doesn't change the house edge. In Royal Aces, the 5th coin is essential to get the boosted Royal Flush bonus. If you aren't playing max bet, the RTP of a Royal Aces machine drops significantly, making it a much worse bet than Craps.

### Skill vs. Luck
Blackjack requires constant decision-making and mathematical precision. Craps is mostly about avoiding the "sucker bets" on the layout. Royal Aces requires a specific strategy that focuses on **Hit Frequency Optimization** for the royal flush. For those looking for **video poker tips for beginners**, Royal Aces is slightly more complex to master than Blackjack but offers a more solitary and relaxed environment.

### RNG vs. Physical Randomness
While Craps relies on physical dice and Blackjack on a physical or shuffled deck, Royal Aces uses **Pseudo-Randomness** through an RNG. This ensures that every hand is fair and independent, just like the roll of the dice in a certified game of Craps.

If you want to know **how to win video poker daily**, understanding these comparisons is vital. Royal Aces offers a great balance of risk and reward, provided you play with perfect strategy and a max bet.
