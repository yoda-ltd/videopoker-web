## The Risk: 10-Coin Multipliers vs. 10-Coin Patterns
Both games require a **10-coin bet per line** (5 coins for the base game, 5 for the feature), but the risk is distributed differently.

- **Hot Roll Poker (The High-Velocity Risk):** The "Risk" in Hot Roll is immediate. Every 6 hands, you roll the dice. If you hit a 12x multiplier and *lose* the hand, your 10-coin bet is gone. The risk is "Point-in-Time"—you are betting on the "Big Roll" and the "Big Hand" happening simultaneously.
- **Bingo Poker (The Grinder's Risk):** The "Risk" in Bingo Poker is a "Long-Term Burn." You are betting 10 coins per hand to fill a 25-square Bingo board. If you walk away before completing a Bingo pattern, you have effectively "Lost" your 5-coin-per-hand investment. The risk is in "Time" and "Persistence."

## The Reward: Explosive Multipliers vs. Structured Jackpots
- **Hot Roll Poker (The Explosive Reward):** The "Reward" is a massive multiplier (up to 12x) applied to any winning hand. A simple Flush (30 coins) becomes a 360-coin win. A Royal Flush with a 12x multiplier is a life-changing payout. The reward is "Uncapped" and dramatic.
- **Bingo Poker (The Structured Reward):** The "Reward" is a fixed "Bingo Jackpot" earned when you complete a pattern on the board. The rewards are more predictable and "Accumulated" over time. The reward is a "Closing Event" that provides a significant bankroll boost.

## Variance Normalization: Smoothing the Spikes
- **Hot Roll Poker:** This is the definition of "High Variance." Your bankroll will move in sharp, vertical spikes and dips. The **Variance Normalization** only happens over thousands of hands because the 12x multiplier is so rare.
- **Bingo Poker:** Offers much better **Variance Normalization** in the short term. Because you are constantly making progress toward a "Bingo," the session feels more "Productive." The patterns act as a "Safety Net" that rewards you for consistent play.

## Side-Bet EV: The Cost of Admission
- **Hot Roll Side-Bet:** You are "Paying for Volatility." The 5-coin side-bet makes the game "Hyper-Action," pushing the RTP to 99%+ through infrequent, massive wins.
- **Bingo Poker Side-Bet:** You are "Paying for Completion." The 5-coin side-bet pushes the RTP to 99%+ by adding "Extra Payouts" to your standard poker wins.

## Feature Hit Frequency: Constant Action vs. Goal Orientation
- **Hot Roll Poker:** High frequency. Dice roll every ~6 hands. The "Action" is constant, keeping the dopamine flowing.
- **Bingo Poker:** Lower frequency for the *payout*, but constant frequency for the *progress*. Every winning hand fills a square, giving the player a sense of "Moving the Needle."
