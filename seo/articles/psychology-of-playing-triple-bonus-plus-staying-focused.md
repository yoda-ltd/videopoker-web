# Psychology of Playing Triple Bonus Plus: Staying Focused

Triple Bonus Plus is a variant of the "Bonus" family of video poker games, offering massive payouts for four-of-a-kind. However, its high variance requires a specific **Pro Mindset** and extreme **Professional Discipline**.

## The High-Stakes Dopamine Loop of Triple Bonus

The "Triple Bonus" in Triple Bonus Plus refers to the massive payouts for Four of a Kind—even larger than Double Double Bonus. This creates an intense **Dopamine Loop** that can be dangerous for players who aren't disciplined.

- **The Dopamine Loop**: The huge reward for hitting four Aces or four 2s, 3s, or 4s triggers a massive neurological response. This can lead to "chasing" behavior where players "hope" for the big hit rather than playing statistically.
- **Expert Video Poker Strategy Tips**: To succeed, you must stick to the math and avoid the "chase." If you start holding Aces when it's not the correct play, your edge is gone.
- **Loss Aversion Bias**: The volatility of Triple Bonus Plus can lead to significant losses in a short time. This triggers **Loss Aversion Bias**, where players become "desperate" to hit a Four of a Kind to "make it all back." 

## Navigating the "Royal Desert" and "Triple Bonus" Droughts

In Triple Bonus Plus, your return is heavily weighted towards the big four-of-a-kind hands. This means the wait for a Royal Flush (the **Royal Desert**) is even more taxing.

1. **Cognitive Resilience**: You must be able to withstand 100,000 hands without a Royal Flush, provided you are hitting enough "Triple Bonus" hands to stay in the game.
2. **The Triple Bonus Drought**: If you go several hours without hitting four-of-a-kind, your bankroll will take a massive hit. This is the "Triple Bonus Grind."
3. **Tilt Mitigation**: When your bankroll is melting, the urge to play faster or "gamble" on draws becomes intense. **Tilt Control** is your only defense.

## Professional Discipline: The Assembly Line of Triple Bonus

To learn **how to become a professional video poker player**, you must treat Triple Bonus Plus as a cold, calculating process.

- **The Assembly Line**: Every hand is just another calculation. There is no "feeling" that the Aces are coming.
- **Session Fatigue**: Triple Bonus Plus has a complex strategy that requires constant attention. **Session Fatigue** after 3-4 hours can lead to costly errors in kicker management or penalty card holds.
- **Pro Mindset**: A pro judges their performance by the correctness of their holds, not by the session total.

## Responsible Play and the Pro Boundary

**Responsible video poker playing** in Triple Bonus Plus requires a massive bankroll and the discipline to walk away when the "edge" is gone.

- **Bankroll Management**: If your bankroll is too small, you will play "scared," which leads to strategy errors.
- **Tilt Control**: If you find yourself angry at the machine for not "giving" you the Triple Bonus payout, you are in a state of Tilt and must stop immediately.

## Conclusion
Triple Bonus Plus is a game of incredible highs and devastating lows. By focusing on **Tilt Control** and maintaining a rigorous **Pro Mindset**, you can harness the power of the "Triple Bonus" and navigate the volatility of this high-octane game.
