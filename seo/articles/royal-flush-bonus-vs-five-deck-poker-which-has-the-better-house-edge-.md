# Royal Flush Bonus vs. Five Deck Poker: Which Has the Better House Edge?

Comparing **Royal Flush Bonus** and **Five Deck Poker** is a study in probability expansion. While Royal Flush Bonus focuses on maximizing the top hand in a standard deck, Five Deck Poker uses five independent decks to create otherwise impossible hands. To choose the right game, you must understand how **video poker fairness and regulation** ensures the integrity of these specialized "Jackpot-Centric" models.

## The Mathematical Framework: Focus vs. Breadth

The house edge in these games is determined by how the mathematical value is "Concentrated."

*   **Royal Flush Bonus:** Value is heavily weighted toward the Royal Flush (10-J-Q-K-A of the same suit). The edge comes from the player's ability to maximize their "Royal EV" through aggressive holds.
*   **Five Deck Poker:** Value is distributed across hands like "Five-of-a-Kind," which are enabled by the five-deck model. The edge comes from the player's ability to master the unique probabilities of duplicate cards.

## House Edge Analysis: The Accuracy of the Math

The accuracy of the advertised edge in these high-value variants is guaranteed by **Return to Player (RTP) Verification** during independent **Lab Audits**.

**The Verdict:** **Royal Flush Bonus** generally offers a superior house edge for skilled players who can handle the extreme volatility. The depth of the "Royal-Hunting" strategy provides more mathematical value than the multi-deck model of Five Deck Poker.
