2.  **4 to a Royal Flush:** You have a 1-in-48 shot at the top jackpot. The mathematical Expectation (EV) makes this a mandatory double.
3.  **4 to a Straight Flush:** Very high equity. 
4.  **4 to a Flush:** You have 9 outs to hit the Flush. The EV of the potential payout outweighs the risk of the extra bet.
5.  **4 to an Open-Ended Straight:** If the straight pays well (e.g., 4-for-1), doubling is often the correct move.
