# High Limit All American: The Whale's Guide
Category: Variant Guide

All American Video Poker is a variant that offers significantly higher payouts for Straights, Flushes, and Straight Flushes, at the cost of lower payouts for Full Houses and Two Pairs. For the "whale," High Limit All American is a game of "Linear Sequences." At the $25, $50, and $100 **Credit Denominations**, a Straight Flush is a massive **Attendant-Paid Jackpot**. ## The Sequence Edge: Volatility Buffering
In All American, a Straight and a Flush often pay 8:1 ($4,000 on a $500 bet). A Straight Flush typically pays 200:1 ($100,000). 

**Volatility Buffering** is mandatory. Because Two Pair only pays 1:1 (money back), you will experience "Linear Slumps" where you aren't hitting the sequences. A professional whale bankroll of 4,000 units ($400,000 for $100 players) is required to sustain the bankroll during these slumps.
