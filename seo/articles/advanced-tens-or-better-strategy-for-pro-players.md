# Advanced Tens or Better Strategy: Pro-Player Mentorship and Team Mechanics

Tens or Better (ToB) is the most conservative variant of video poker. It pays out on a pair of 10s or better, providing even more frequent "Churn" than Jacks or Better. For the professional, ToB is the "Stability Engine"—the game used for low-risk bankroll building and maximum comp accumulation. Mastering **how to become a professional video poker player** requires understanding the "High-Volume, Low-Variance" nature of Tens or Better.

## The Mechanics of Video Poker Teams: Team Variance Reduction

While ToB has the lowest variance in the casino, it still requires **pro video poker team strategy** for maximum efficiency.

### Bankroll Pooling in ToB
- **The Rationale:** Even in ToB, a "Royal Flush Drought" can last 40,000 hands.
- **The Solution:** A team uses **Bankroll Pooling**. By having five players grind 6/5 ToB (99.1% return), the team effectively "Shortens the Royal Cycle."
- **Team Variance Reduction:** The extremely frequent wins from Pairs of 10s, Jacks, Queens, Kings, and Aces provide the "fuel" for the team to stay in the game indefinitely.

## Scouting Logic: Finding the "Full Pay" 6/5 ToB

Professional ToB players are masters of **Information Arbitrage**. The 6/5 table (99.1%) is the *only* table a pro will play.

1. **Scouting Networks:** Pros pay scouts to find the rare 6/5 ToB machines. Many casinos have downgraded to 5/5 or 5/4, which are "Unplayable" for a pro.
2. **Scouting for Progressives:** Often, ToB is part of a "Progressive Bank." A pro team will monitor the Royal Flush jackpot. When it exceeds $1,500 (on a 25c machine), the game becomes **Positive EV** (>100%), and the team will deploy a **Session Relay**.

## Deep Dive: EV Maximization and the "ToB" Inversion

ToB strategy is even more conservative than JoB.

### The "10s" Inversion
- **Standard Play:** Discard 10s.
- **ToB Pro Play:** A professional knows that a "Pair of 10s" is a guaranteed win. They will hold a pair of 10s over a single Ace or King every time.
- **EV Maximization:** The goal is the "Guaranteed Return." By holding the 10s, the pro maintains the bankroll for the next 1,000 hands.

## Professional Mentorship: Learning the "ToB Grind"

**Professional Mentorship** in ToB is about "Efficiency."

- **Session Relay:** Teaching how to hand off a positive-EV ToB machine to a teammate to ensure the team "Owns" the machine until the Royal hits.
