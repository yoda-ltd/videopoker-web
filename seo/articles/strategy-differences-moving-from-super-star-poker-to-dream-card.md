## Specific Strategy Shifts: When Dream Card Logic Overrides Standard Priorities

### 2. High Pair vs. 4-to-a-Straight/Flush
In standard Super Star Poker strategy, a High Pair (Jacks or Better) almost always beats a 4-card Straight or Flush draw.
- **Dream Card Shift:** If the Dream Card gives you the 4th card to a Flush, the **Accumulated Multipliers** and high payout potential of the completed hand often outweigh the safety of the High Pair. The "Dream Card logic" effectively forces you to play more aggressively for high-value hands because the "missing link" has already been provided.

### 3. Discarding the "Kicker"

If you have three 4s and the Dream Card gives you an Ace (the kicker for a top-tier payout in DDB), the hand is already "Pat." You no longer need to calculate the "Kicker EV" yourself; you simply hold the gifts the machine gives you.
