# Super Aces Bonus vs. Bonus Poker Deluxe: Which Has the Better House Edge?

Comparing **Super Aces Bonus** and **Bonus Poker Deluxe** is a comparison of tiered bonuses against a simplified "Flat" bonus structure. While Super Aces Bonus rewards you massively for targeting Aces, Bonus Poker Deluxe pays the same high amount for any Four-of-a-Kind. To choose the right game, you must understand how **video poker fairness and regulation** ensures the integrity of these specialized "Bonus-Centric" models.

## The Mathematical Framework: Focus vs. Uniformity

The house edge in these games is determined by how the "Big Wins" are distributed across the ranks.

*   **Super Aces Bonus:** Value is heavily concentrated at the top. Four Aces typically pays 400-for-1 (2,000 coins at max bet), while other quads pay significantly less. The edge is tied to your ability to prioritize Aces in your strategic holds.
*   **Bonus Poker Deluxe:** Value is distributed evenly across all quads. Every Four-of-a-Kind (Aces through 2s) pays a flat 80-for-1. The edge is tied to a more balanced strategy that treats all matching cards with equal respect.
