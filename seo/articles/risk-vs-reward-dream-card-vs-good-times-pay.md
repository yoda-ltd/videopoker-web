### Strategic Divergence: Extra Card vs. Multipliers

In **Dream Card Poker**, the reward is concentrated in the randomly awarded "Dream Card" that turns a mediocre hand into a winner. This is a game of **EV Maximization** through wild card features. 2.  **Good Times Pay Reward:** A consistent multiplier excitement that extends over several hands.
3.  **Bankroll Requirement:** Both games require a large bankroll, but Good Times Pay is slightly more "swingy" due to the persistent multipliers.
