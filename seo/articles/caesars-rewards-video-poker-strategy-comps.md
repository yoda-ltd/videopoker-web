## The Mechanics of Caesars Rewards for Video Poker

Unlike some "transparent" locals programs, Caesars uses a more opaque system that heavily weights your **ADT (Average Daily Theoretical)**.

### 1. Earning Tier Credits
In 2026, the standard earn rate for video poker is **1 Tier Credit (TC) for every $10 coin-in**. 
*   **The Pro Tip:** Always look for "Tier Credit Multiplier" events. Caesars frequently runs 5x or 10x multiplier days. A "Diamond in a Day" (DIAD) run during a 10x multiplier requires significantly less bankroll risk.

### 2. Reward Credits (RC)
Reward Credits are your "spendable" currency. 100 RC = $1.00. While the earn rate is the same as TCs, the value is in the **Bounce Back** offers. If you put in a high-volume session (high ADT), you will likely receive a mailer with "Reward Credits" or "Free Play" to entice you to return.

## Deep Dive: Theo vs. Reality
The "Theo" (Theoretical Loss) is what Caesars uses to determine your mailers. 
*   **Formula:** Coin-In × House Edge = Theo.
*   **The Strategy:** Caesars "sees" video poker as having a higher house edge than it actually does if you play perfectly. By playing a 99.5% Jacks or Better machine, your actual loss is 0.5%, but the casino might "rate" you at 2%. This "Theo Gap" is where you earn **best video poker rewards programs** value.

## Optimizing for RFB (Room, Food, Beverage)
To secure "Full RFB," you need to maintain a consistent **ADT**. 
1.  **Concentrate Play:** It is better to play $5,000 coin-in in one day than $1,000 coin-in over five days. The former gives you a high ADT; the latter marks you as a "low-value" tourist.
2.  **The "No Play" Day:** If you are staying on a comped room, never swipe your card on a day you don't intend to play heavily. A "zero" or "low" day will tank your ADT and kill your future **Mailer Logic**.
