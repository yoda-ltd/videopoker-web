# Pro Player Interview: The Online Multi-Tabler on Efficiency and Data

In the final part of our series on **how to become a professional video poker player**, we talk to "Sarah," a digital nomad who earns her living playing online. Sarah represents the modern "Advantage Player" (AP) who focuses on volume, speed, and mathematical precision.

## The Digital Edge: Efficiency and Multi-Tabling

**Interviewer:** Sarah, how do you manage to stay profitable in the competitive online space?

**Sarah:** It’s all about efficiency. I don't play one hand at a time. I'm usually multi-tabling across three or four different casinos. My goal is maximum hands-per-hour. By increasing my volume, I reach the "Long Run" much faster, which reduces the impact of short-term variance.

## Advanced Strategy: EV Maximization and Bonus Hunting

**Interviewer:** What games do you focus on?

**Sarah:** I look for games with high base RTP, but more importantly, I look for "Deposit Bonuses" and "Loyalty Multipliers." Online **EV Maximization** is about stacking the base return of the game with the "Expected Value" of the bonus. If I play a 99.5% game with a 1% cashback bonus, I'm at a 100.5% return.

## The Transition: Bankroll Sizing and Emotional Resilience

**Interviewer:** What was your path to becoming a pro?

**Sarah:** I started as a hobbyist, but I was always obsessed with the data. The transition required strict **bankroll sizing**. You need a dedicated "bankroll" that is completely separate from your living expenses. 

**Emotional resilience** is also huge. When you're playing at home alone, a losing streak can feel very isolating. You don't have the "free drinks" or the casino atmosphere to distract you. You have to be okay with the "grind."

## Pro Video Poker Player Secrets: Networking and Tools

**Interviewer:** Do you use any special tools?

**Sarah:** I use simulators to refine my strategy for every possible variation of a pay table. But the real "tool" is **networking with other APs**. We have private Discord servers where we discuss which sites have fast payouts and which ones are "stiffing" players on bonuses. 

## Final Advice on How to Become a Professional Video Poker Player

**Interviewer:** What’s your #1 tip for someone starting out today?

**Sarah:** Don't guess. If you don't know the mathematically "correct" hold for a hand, look it up. There are no "hunches" in professional play. That is the core of all **pro video poker player secrets**: the math never lies, but your gut will.
