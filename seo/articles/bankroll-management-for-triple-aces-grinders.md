# Bankroll Management for Triple Aces Grinders

Triple Aces is a high-action video poker variant that offers massive payouts for—you guessed it—three or four Aces. Because so much of the **Theoretical Return** is concentrated in these specific hands, the **Variance** is extremely high.

### The Grinder's Bankroll
For a Triple Aces grinder, **Bankroll Management** is not just a suggestion; it is a survival requirement. Because the "dry spells" can be long, you need significant **Variance Buffering**. A bankroll of 1,000 to 2,000 units is often recommended for a single session to ensure **Session Sustainability**.

### Is Max Bet Always Better?
A common niche question is: "Is max bet always better for bankroll longevity?" In Triple Aces, the answer is a categorical yes. Not only does the Royal Flush require 5 coins for the bonus, but the bonus payouts for the Aces are often scaled to favor the maximum bet. If you can't afford the max bet at a certain denomination, move down.

### Hit Frequency Optimization
In Triple Aces, you are often tempted to hold an Ace even when the strategy says otherwise. Resist this. **Hit Frequency Optimization** in this game depends on maintaining your bankroll with smaller wins while waiting for the "Aces" to land. Deviating from the strategy to chase Aces is the fastest way to bust.

### Beginner Tips
If you are looking for **video poker tips for beginners**, Triple Aces might be a bit overwhelming due to the swings. Start with Jacks or Better to learn the ropes. If you are determined to learn **how to win video poker daily**, you must master the discipline of Triple Aces and never let your emotions dictate your holds.

### RNG and Fairness
Remember that the machine operates on **Pseudo-Randomness**. Just because you haven't seen an Ace in 50 hands doesn't mean one is "due." Every hand is a fresh start from a randomized 52-card deck.

Triple Aces is a game of "all or nothing." If you have the bankroll and the discipline, it can be incredibly lucrative. If you don't, it will be a very short session.
