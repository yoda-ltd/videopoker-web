# Comparing Tens or Better to Blackjack and Craps

Tens or Better is a video poker variant where the lowest-paying hand is a pair of 10s. This makes it a very low-variance game. But how does it compare to the classic table games?

### RTP Comparison
- **Tens or Better (6/5)**: Offers a **Theoretical Return** of 99.14% with perfect strategy.
- **Blackjack**: Offers a 99.5% RTP (0.5% house edge) with perfect basic strategy.
- **Craps (Pass Line)**: Offers a 98.59% RTP (1.41% house edge).

While Blackjack still offers the best base odds, Tens or Better is significantly better than a standard game of Craps and offers a much smoother experience for your bankroll.

### Variance and Sustainability
Tens or Better has extremely low **Variance**, making it one of the best games for **Session Sustainability**. Because you win more frequently (on any pair of 10s or higher), your bankroll lasts longer. This requires much less **Variance Buffering** than you would for a standard game of Blackjack.

### "Is Max Bet Always Better for Bankroll Longevity?"
In Tens or Better, the answer is yes. The 5th coin is still essential for the Royal Flush bonus. For those looking for **video poker tips for beginners**, our advice is always the same: play max bet or find a different game. Without the 5th coin, the RTP of Tens or Better falls below that of a standard game of Craps.

### Skill and Strategy
The strategy for Tens or Better is very similar to Jacks or Better but slightly easier because of the added value of the 10s. It focuses on **Hit Frequency Optimization** for the low-paying hands. It is much easier to master than Blackjack's basic strategy and significantly more engaging than the decisions in Craps.

### RNG Reality
Tens or Better uses **Pseudo-Randomness** through an RNG to determine the cards. This ensures the game is fair and every hand is independent. If you want to know **how to win video poker daily**, understanding this and sticking to your strategy is the only way to manage your bankroll effectively.

Tens or Better is a fantastic game for those who find Blackjack too stressful or Craps too random. If you want a slow, relaxing session with a high return, this is the game for you.
