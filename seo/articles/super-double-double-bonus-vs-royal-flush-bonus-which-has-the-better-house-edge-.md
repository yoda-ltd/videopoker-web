# Super Double Double Bonus vs. Royal Flush Bonus: Which Has the Better House Edge?

Comparing **Super Double Double Bonus (SDDB)** and **Royal Flush Bonus** is a comparison of two ways to target the top of the pay table. While SDDB focuses on multiple high-value Four-of-a-Kind combinations with kickers, Royal Flush Bonus concentrates its value in a single, rarest hand. To choose the right game, you must understand how **video poker fairness and regulation** ensures the integrity of these specialized "Jackpot-Centric" models.

## The Mathematical Framework: Multiple Quads vs. One Royal

The house edge in these games is determined by the "Concentration of Value."

*   **Super Double Double Bonus:** Value is spread across several high-paying quads (Aces through 4s with kickers). The edge comes from the player's ability to master the complex "Kicker-Targeting" strategy across multiple ranks.
*   **Royal Flush Bonus:** Value is heavily concentrated in the Royal Flush hit (800-for-1+). The edge comes from the player's ability to maximize their "Royal EV" through aggressive holds, often breaking high-paying hands to chase the jackpot.
