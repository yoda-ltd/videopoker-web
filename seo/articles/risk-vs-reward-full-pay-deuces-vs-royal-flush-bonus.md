# Risk vs. Reward: Full Pay Deuces vs. Royal Flush Bonus
Category: Comparison

Choosing between **Full Pay Deuces** and **Royal Flush Bonus** involves balancing the rewards of "Multi-Wild Card Connectivity" against the "Targeted Jackpot" tiers of Royal Flush Bonus. This creates a significant **Strategic Divergence** in how you approach **Volatility Normalization**.

| Feature | Full Pay Deuces (100.76%) | Royal Flush Bonus (Full Pay) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **RTP (Theoretical)** | 100.76% | 99.80% |
| **Variance Index** | 25.8 | 32.0 |
| **Reward Focus** | Wild Card Connectivity (Deuces) | Targeted Royal Flush Jackpot |
| **House Edge Analysis** | -0.76% (Player Edge) | 0.20% |

### Strategic Divergence: Wilds vs. Royal Jackpot

In **Full Pay Deuces**, the four 2s are your most powerful tools, allowing you to complete hands that would otherwise be worthless. This is a game of **EV Maximization** through wild card connectivity. In **Royal Flush Bonus**, the game is natural, but the payout for a Royal Flush is significantly increased. This leads to a higher **Volatility Normalization** (32.0 vs 25.8).

**Strategy Overlap:** There is roughly 40% overlap. In Full Pay Deuces, you will hold a single 2 almost as if it were a high pair. In Royal Flush Bonus, you prioritize Royal Flush cards above all else, often breaking pat winners to maximize the bonus potential. This is the essence of **Payout Compression**—the 2s in Deuces Wild and the Royal in Royal Flush Bonus dominate their respective EV landscapes.

### Key Risk/Reward Factors
1.  **Full Pay Deuces Reward:** A significant player advantage (0.76%) with a stable return through wild cards.
2.  **Royal Flush Bonus Reward:** A superior "Jackpot" excitement with frequent large payouts for a Royal Flush.
3.  **Strategy Complexity:** Both games reward those who can master the specialized holds for the highest payouts.

The **best video poker strategy comparison** indicates that Full Pay Deuces is the superior game for the player looking for the best overall return, while Royal Flush Bonus is better for those who enjoy the excitement of jackpot hunting. A **video poker house edge analysis** confirms that both are elite choices for those who master their respective strategies.
