# How to Play Deuces Wild Video Poker: A Comprehensive Guide

Deuces Wild is a "wild card" video poker game that is among the most popular variants in both online and land-based casinos. Because the four 2s (Deuces) in the deck are wild, you can form much higher-value hands (like Five of a Kind) much more frequently than in Jacks or Better.

## Deuces Wild: The Rules of the Game

### 1. The Deck and the Deal
Deuces Wild uses a standard **52-card deck**. There are no jokers.
*   The game begins when you place your bet (usually 1 to 5 coins).
*   You are dealt **five cards** face up.

### 2. The Draw
*   You choose which cards to **Hold** and which to **Discard**.
*   The discarded cards are replaced by new cards from the same deck.
*   The resulting five-card hand is your final hand.

### 3. The "Wild" Cards (The Deuces)
Every **2 (Deuce)** in the deck is a wild card. A deuce can represent **any rank and any suit** that will result in the highest possible payout for your hand. 

## Deuces Wild Hand Rankings (Lowest to Highest)

Because of the wild cards, the hand rankings are different from standard poker. In Deuces Wild, the **minimum winning hand is Three of a Kind**.

1.  **Three of a Kind:** Three cards of the same rank. (Pays 1-for-1).
2.  **Straight:** Five cards in sequence.
3.  **Flush:** Five cards of the same suit.
4.  **Full House:** Three cards of one rank and two of another.
5.  **Four of a Kind:** Four cards of the same rank.
6.  **Straight Flush:** Five cards in sequence and of the same suit.
7.  **Five of a Kind:** Five cards of the same rank (e.g., four 8s and a Deuce).
8.  **Wild Royal Flush:** A 10-J-Q-K-A of the same suit using one or more deuces.
9.  **Four Deuces:** All four wild cards in one hand.
10. **Natural Royal Flush:** A 10-J-Q-K-A of the same suit with NO deuces.

## Basic Strategy: The "Golden Rules"

To maximize your Return to Player (RTP), you must follow these basic guidelines:

### If You Are Dealt Deuces:
*   **4 Deuces:** Hold all four. (Discard the 5th card).
*   **3 Deuces:** Hold a Royal Flush or Five of a Kind. If you have neither, keep only the deuces.
*   **2 Deuces:** Hold any Four of a Kind or better. Keep any 4-card Royal Flush draw. Otherwise, keep only the two deuces.
*   **1 Deuce:** Hold any winning hand (Three of a Kind or better). Keep any 3-card Royal Flush draw. Otherwise, keep only the one deuce.

### If You Are Dealt NO Deuces:
*   **Never hold a lone high card** (Jack, Queen, King, Ace). They have no value in Deuces Wild.
*   **Hold any pair** (but only one pair).
*   **Hold any 4-card Flush or Straight draw.**
*   **Discard all five cards** if you have no deuce and no pair.
