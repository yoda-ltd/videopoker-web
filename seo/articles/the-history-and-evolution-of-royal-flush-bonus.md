# The History and Evolution of Royal Flush Bonus Video Poker

While every video poker game is technically a "Royal Flush" game, Royal Flush Bonus is a specific variant that changed the mathematical landscape of the casino floor. This article explores the history of how this game turned the rarest hand in poker into a structured "Bonus" mechanic.

## The "Mega-Jackpot" Era

In the early days of video poker, the Royal Flush was always a fixed payout: 250-for-1 (if betting 1-4 coins) or 800-for-1 (if betting 5 coins). In the 1990s, however, casinos began looking for ways to create "Mega-Jackpots" that could compete with the multi-million dollar "Wheel of Fortune" slot machines.

Royal Flush Bonus was born from this desire. Instead of a fixed payout, it introduced a "Bonus" pool that would grow—or be triggered by specific conditions—making the Royal Flush even more valuable than usual.

## The Evolution of the "Progressive" Mechanic

Royal Flush Bonus helped pioneer the "Progressive Jackpot" in video poker:
1.  **Linked Progressives:** Multiple machines were linked together, with a small percentage of every "Coin-In" going toward a massive Royal Flush jackpot.
2.  **Sequential Royals:** Some versions of Royal Flush Bonus evolved to pay an even larger "Super Bonus" if the Royal Flush appeared in a specific order (A-K-Q-J-10).
3.  **The "Bonus" Bet:** Later versions introduced an optional side-bet. If you placed the extra bet and hit a Royal, you would receive a "Bonus" payout that could be double or triple the standard 4,000-coin win.

## Essential Terms for Royal Flush Bonus Players

When consulting the complete video poker glossary, these are the video poker terms every player should know for this variant:

*   **Bankroll:** Progressive and Bonus Royal games have the highest variance in the industry. You are "playing for the jackpot," which means you must be prepared for long stretches of losing play.
*   **Expected Value (EV):** The EV of a Royal Flush Bonus machine changes every second. As the progressive jackpot grows, the EV increases. Professional "Jackpot Hunters" will only play when the bonus pool reaches a "Break-Even" point where the player has a mathematical edge.
*   **Action:** These machines generate massive "Action" for casinos because players are lured in by the "Life-Changing" potential of the bonus.

## The Strategic Shift

The history of Royal Flush Bonus is also the history of "Jackpot Strategy." Players learned that as the bonus grew, it became mathematically correct to "break" almost any other winning hand—even a Straight Flush—if there was a chance to draw for the Royal. This "Jackpot-only" mindset is a unique evolution in the video poker world.

## Royal Flush Bonus Today

Today, these mechanics are found in "Wide Area Progressives" (WAP) like *Millioniser* or *Mega-Poker*. While the base game is usually a "Short Pay" version of Jacks or Better, the "Royal Flush Bonus" ensures that every spin has the potential to be a legendary win. It remains the ultimate expression of the "High Risk, High Reward" philosophy in gaming.
