## The Professional Terminology of the Comp Hustle

*   **The Strategy:** The casino uses your ADT to determine your "worth." If your ADT is $500, they are willing to give you about $150-$200 (30-40%) back in comps (rooms, food, free play).

### 2. Theo (Theoretical Loss)
"Theo" is what the casino *expects* to win from you. 
*   **The Theo Gap:** A professional video poker player plays with a 0.5% house edge (or better). The casino, however, might "rate" that same machine at a 2.0% house edge. This 1.5% "Theo Gap" is where your profit comes from in the form of comps.

### 3. Bounce Back and Mailer Logic
"Bounce Back" is the free play or resort credit you get in the mail *after* your trip. 
*   **Mailer Logic:** Casinos use complex algorithms to determine your mailers. Consistent, high-volume sessions (high ADT) trigger the best offers. A single "zero play" day during a 3-night stay can kill your mailers for months.
