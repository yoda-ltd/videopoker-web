# Strategy Differences: Moving from Super Double Bonus to Triple Aces Poker

Triple Aces Poker is a "volatile" cousin of Super Double Bonus (SDB) that rewards players who focus exclusively on the high-end of the pay table. This is where **Strategy Drift** becomes most dangerous for a casual player. While SDB focuses on a broader range of Four of a Kind payouts, Triple Aces is laser-focused on the Aces.

## The Super Double Bonus Baseline

In Super Double Bonus, your **Baseline Hand Ranking** focuses on achieving four of any card from Ace to Five. However, when transitioning to Triple Aces Poker, your strategy must include **Volatility Scaling**. You are no longer just looking for a broad range of Four of a Kind; you are specifically hunting for the "Triple Aces" payout.

## Strategy Portability: The 80/20 Rule

Transitioning from Super Double Bonus to Triple Aces, you'll find that **75% of your strategy remains the same** for most mid-tier hands (Pairs, Flushes, and Straights). The **25% that changes** is entirely focused on the "Ace hunt." This 25% shift causes the most **Bankroll Drain** if not corrected.

### The 25% "Bankroll Drain" Zone:
1.  **Three Aces Discarding:** In Super Double Bonus, you might hold a low kicker with three Aces. In Triple Aces, you should always discard the kicker to draw for the massive fourth Ace payout.
2.  **Full House Devaluation:** A Full House in Triple Aces often pays less (9-for-1 or 8-for-1) than in Super Double Bonus (10-for-1). This makes you even more likely to break a Full House to draw for a fourth Ace.
3.  **Two Pair Discarding:** You should frequently discard one pair from a Two Pair (Aces over anything) to draw for the fourth Ace, as the Two Pair payout is only 1-for-1.

## EV Erosion and High Volatility

To **play video poker like a pro**, you must understand that Triple Aces has some of the highest volatility in the casino. Your **EV Erosion** happens when you play "too safe." If you don't take the aggressive draws for the 4-Aces, you are literally throwing money away over the long term.

## Summary of Modifications
*   **Hold:** Be extremely aggressive with any lone Ace.
*   **Modify:** Be prepared to break more "made" hands in favor of the massive jackpot draws.
*   **Target:** Focus exclusively on the top tier of the pay table; the middle tiers are just there to keep you in the game until the big hit.

This best video poker strategy comparison shows that Triple Aces is not for the faint of heart, but with the right strategy, it can be the most rewarding variant in the house.
