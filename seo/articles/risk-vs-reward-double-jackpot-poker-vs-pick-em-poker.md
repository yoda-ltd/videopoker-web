2.  **Pick 'em Poker Reward:** A potential player advantage with extremely low volatility, making it one of the most stable games in the casino.
3.  **Strategy Simplicity:** Pick 'em Poker requires much less mental effort for **Volatility Normalization**.

The **best video poker strategy comparison** indicates that Pick 'em Poker is the superior game for the player who wants the highest return with minimum risk, while Double Jackpot is better for those who enjoy tiered bonuses.
