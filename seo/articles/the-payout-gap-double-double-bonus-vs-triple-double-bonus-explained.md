# The Payout Gap: Double Double Bonus vs Triple Double Bonus Explained

In the world of **best odds video poker**, players often find themselves choosing between two of the most popular machines on the floor: Double Double Bonus and Triple Double Bonus. While they look similar, the **EV Gap** between them depends entirely on their specific pay tables and your tolerance for **House Edge Variance**.

## The Best Odds Index: 9/6 Double Double Bonus vs 9/7 Triple Double Bonus
In the **highest return video poker games** 2026 ranking, both machines offer strong professional returns when found in their "Full Pay" configurations:
*   **9/6 Double Double Bonus:** 98.98% RTP.
*   **9/7 Triple Double Bonus:** 99.58% RTP.

At first glance, Triple Double Bonus appears to be the superior choice. However, Triple Double Bonus offers a massive "Bonus" for hitting Four of a Kind with a kicker (specifically Four Aces with a 2, 3, or 4 kicker, which pays 800-for-1 instead of 400-for-1). This change in the pay table structure shifts the **Theoretical Return Convergence**.

## Analysis of "Short Pay" Predation
The real "Payout Gap" occurs when casinos offer sub-optimal versions of these games. 
*   **Double Double Bonus (7/5):** RTP falls to 95.71%.
*   **Triple Double Bonus (8/5):** RTP falls to 95.97%.

If you find a 7/5 Double Double Bonus machine next to an 8/5 Triple Double Bonus machine, the 0.26% **EV Gap** is predatory. A professional player would never touch the 7/5 Double Double Bonus machine in this scenario. However, if the choice is between 7/5 Double Double Bonus (95.71%) and 9/7 Triple Double Bonus (99.58%), the Triple Double Bonus machine is actually the much better bet.

## House Edge Variance and Strategy
Triple Double Bonus has significantly higher variance than Double Double Bonus. This is because a massive portion of the return is tied up in the Four Aces with a kicker payout (800-for-1). If you don't hit this hand, your bankroll will drain much faster than in Double Double Bonus. 
*   **Double Double Bonus Strategy:** Focuses on bankroll preservation and hitting the Four of a Kind with a kicker.
*   **Triple Double Bonus Strategy:** Aggressively pursues the Four Aces with a kicker.
