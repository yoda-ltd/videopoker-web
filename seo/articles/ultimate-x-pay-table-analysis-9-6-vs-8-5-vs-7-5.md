# Ultimate X Pay Table Analysis: 9/6 vs 8/5 vs 7/5

In the world of professional video poker, the pay table is your roadmap to profit. In Ultimate X, the difference between a 9/6 (Full House/Flush) and a 7/5 pay table is even more dramatic than in standard poker due to the way multipliers are calculated.

## Why 9/6 Matters for EV Preservation
The "9/6" refers to the payout for a Full House (9 coins per coin bet) and a Flush (6 coins per coin bet). 
*   **Multiplier Trigger:** In Ultimate X, the Full House and Flush are often the primary triggers for high **Accumulated Multipliers** (10x, 11x, or 12x). 
*   **The Double Hit:** A lower payout on the base hand (like in a 7/5 game) reduces your immediate win *and* makes it harder to justify the 10-coin premium you pay for the **Next-Hand Value**.
