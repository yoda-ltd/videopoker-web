# Tens or Better vs. Bonus Poker Deluxe: Which Has the Better House Edge?

Comparing **Tens or Better** and **Bonus Poker Deluxe** is a comparison of low-volatility stability vs. Quad-focused rewards. While Tens or Better lowers the barrier to a winning hand, Bonus Poker Deluxe increases the reward for every Four-of-a-Kind. To choose the right game, you must understand how **video poker fairness and regulation** ensures the integrity of these specialized mathematical models.

## The Mathematical Framework: Frequency vs. High Payouts

The house edge in these games is determined by how the "Return" is structured across the hand rankings.

*   **Tens or Better:** Value is spread across frequent, small wins. By paying for a pair of 10s, the game ensures a higher "Hit Frequency," keeping your bankroll stable during long sessions.
*   **Bonus Poker Deluxe:** Value is concentrated in hitting Four-of-a-Kind. Every Quad (Aces through 2s) pays a flat 80-for-1 (400 coins at max bet). This requires a more aggressive strategy to realize the full return.
