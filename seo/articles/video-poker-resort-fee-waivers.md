## 1. The Cost of the "Free" Room

It is a common irony in Las Vegas: a player receives a "Comped Room" mailer, only to be charged $250 in resort fees at checkout for a 5-night stay. 

*   **The Math:** $50 (Fee) + $6.69 (Tax) = $56.69 per night.
*   **The Annual Impact:** For a player who spends 30 nights a year in Vegas, that's **$1,700** in fees. For a "Vegas Loop" pro living 365 days a year on the Strip, it's over **$20,000**.

*   **Requirement:** 15,000 Tier Credits (earned at $10 coin-in = 1 TC on video poker).

*   **Strategy:** MGM Tier Credits are harder to earn on video poker alone. You may need to supplement your play with dining and hotel spend to hit Gold.
