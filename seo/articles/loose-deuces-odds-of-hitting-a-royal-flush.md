# Loose Deuces Odds of Hitting a Royal Flush

Loose Deuces is a popular high-volatility variant of Deuces Wild. While standard Deuces Wild variants focus on a balanced pay table, Loose Deuces pumps up the payout for **Four Deuces** to a staggering **500-for-1** (2,500 coins on a 5-coin bet). This shift in the math changes how you should approach the Natural Royal Flush.

## The Odds: Natural Royal Flush vs. Four Deuces

In most video poker games, the Natural Royal Flush is the supreme hand. In Loose Deuces, it still pays the standard **800-for-1**, but the increased value of the Four Deuces (500:1 vs the 200:1 found in Full Pay Deuces) means your strategy revolves around catching 2s more than any other game.

### Probability Statistics
*   **Natural Royal Flush Frequency:** Approximately **1 in 46,100 hands**.
*   **Four Deuces Frequency:** Approximately **1 in 4,800 hands**.

Because Four Deuces occurs nearly 10 times as often as a Natural Royal Flush and pays 62.5% of its value, the Four Deuces hand is actually the primary driver of your **100.97% Expected Value (EV)** in the "Full Pay" version of Loose Deuces.

## Strategy: Holding for the Royal Flush
In Loose Deuces, the "Value of a Deuce" is at its peak. This impacts how you chase a Royal Flush.

1.  **Never Discard a Deuce:** Even if you have 4 cards to a Natural Royal Flush and a Deuce, you keep the Deuce. The EV of holding the Deuce and drawing for Four Deuces is higher.
2.  **3-Card Royal vs. Deuce:** If you have a single Deuce, you keep ONLY the Deuce and discard the other 4 cards (including Royal pieces), unless you have a made hand like Four of a Kind.
3.  **Inside Straight Flushes:** Unlike other games, in Loose Deuces, you almost never chase an inside straight flush if it means discarding potential for a Deuce draw.

## The Volatility Factor
Loose Deuces has a **Volatility Index (VI)** significantly higher than Jacks or Better or even standard Deuces Wild. 
*   **Dry Spells:** Because the Four of a Kind and Full House payouts are suppressed, your bankroll will deplete faster.
*   **The Jackpot Jump:** You are effectively playing for two different "mini-jackpots" (Four Deuces and the Royal).
