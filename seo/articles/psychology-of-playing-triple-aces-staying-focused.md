# Psychology of Playing Triple Aces: Staying Focused

Triple Aces is a variant of video poker that offers massive payouts for Three of a Kind in Aces, with even higher payouts for Four Aces. This creates a unique psychological landscape that requires a specific **Pro Mindset** and intense **Professional Discipline**.

## The Triple Ace Trap: The Dopamine Loop of Triple Aces

In Triple Aces, the extra rewards for Three of a Kind in Aces can lead to a powerful **Dopamine Loop**. 

- **The Dopamine Loop**: The "Triple Bonus" for Aces is designed to trigger a stronger neurochemical response when you hit the big hand.
- **Expert Video Poker Strategy Tips**: To succeed, you must avoid the "Chasing" mentality. Do not hold single Aces in Triple Aces because you "feel" like more Aces are coming. Stick to the mathematically correct play every time.
- **Loss Aversion Bias**: Players who lose back-to-back sessions in Triple Aces are more likely to "Tilt" because they feel they "should" have hit more Aces by now. 

## Handling the "Royal Desert" and "Triple Ace" Droughts

The Royal Flush is still the largest payout in Triple Aces, but the game is less reliant on it than other variants. However, going on a **Royal Desert** can still be a major psychological blow.

1. **Cognitive Resilience**: You must be able to withstand 100,000 hands without a Royal Flush, provided you are hitting enough Three and Four of a Kind in Aces to keep your bankroll healthy.
2. **The Triple Ace Drought**: If you go several hours without hitting Three of a Kind in Aces, your bankroll will take a massive hit. This is the "Triple Aces Grind."
3. **Tilt Mitigation**: When your bankroll is melting, the urge to play faster or "gamble" on draws becomes intense. **Tilt Control** is your only defense.

## Professional Discipline: The Assembly Line Approach

To learn **how to become a professional video poker player**, you must treat Triple Aces like an assembly line. 

- **The Assembly Line**: Every hand is a transaction. There is no "feeling" that the Aces are coming.
- **Session Fatigue**: Because the strategy for Triple Aces differs from Jacks or Better, **Session Fatigue** is a major risk. A single error can wipe out your entire hourly edge.
- **Pro Mindset**: A pro judges their performance by the correctness of their holds, not by the session total.

## Responsible Play and the Pro Boundary

**Responsible video poker playing** in Triple Aces means recognizing when the bonus payouts for Aces are causing you to take the strategy less seriously.

- **Bankroll is King**: If your bankroll is too small, you will play "scared," which leads to strategy errors.
- **Tilt Control**: If you find yourself angry at the machine for not "giving" you the Aces, you are in a state of Tilt and must stop immediately.

## Conclusion
Triple Aces is a game of highs and lows. By focusing on **Tilt Control** and maintaining a rigorous **Pro Mindset**, you can harness the power of the "Triple Aces" and maintain your professional edge in this classic game.
