# Comparing Aces and Faces to Blackjack and Craps

Aces and Faces is a popular video poker variant that offers bonus payouts for Four-of-a-Kind hands involving Aces or Face cards. But how does it compare to the classic table games like Blackjack and Craps?

### The RTP Battle
- **Aces and Faces (9/6)**: Offers a **Theoretical Return** of 99.26% with perfect strategy.
- **Blackjack**: Typically offers a 99.5% RTP (0.5% house edge) with perfect basic strategy.
- **Craps (Pass Line)**: Offers a 98.59% RTP (1.41% house edge).

While Blackjack has a slightly higher base RTP, Aces and Faces provides the chance for a massive payout (the Royal Flush) that Blackjack simply doesn't offer on a single bet.

### Variance and Sustainability
Aces and Faces has higher **Variance** than both Blackjack and Craps because the pay table is "top-heavy" with bonus payouts. To achieve **Session Sustainability**, you need a larger bankroll for Aces and Faces than you would for a slow-paced game of Blackjack. Without proper **Variance Buffering**, the swings of Aces and Faces can be punishing.

### The Max Bet Factor
"Is max bet always better for bankroll longevity?" In Blackjack and Craps, your bet size doesn't change the house edge. In Aces and Faces, the 5th coin is essential to get the full Royal Flush bonus. If you aren't playing max bet, the RTP of an Aces and Faces machine drops significantly, making it a much worse bet than Craps.

### Skill vs. Luck
Blackjack requires constant decision-making and mathematical precision. Craps is mostly about avoiding the "sucker bets" on the layout. Aces and Faces requires a specific strategy that focuses on **Hit Frequency Optimization** for the bonus hands. For those looking for **video poker tips for beginners**, Aces and Faces is slightly more complex to master than Blackjack but offers a more solitary and relaxed environment.

### RNG vs. Physical Randomness
While Craps relies on physical dice and Blackjack on a physical or shuffled deck, Aces and Faces uses **Pseudo-Randomness** through an RNG. This ensures that every hand is fair and independent, just like the roll of the dice in a certified game of Craps.

If you want to know **how to win video poker daily**, understanding these comparisons is vital. Aces and Faces offers a great balance of risk and reward, provided you play with perfect strategy and a max bet.
