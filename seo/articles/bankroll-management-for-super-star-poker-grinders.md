# Bankroll Management for Super Star Poker Grinders

Super Star Poker is the ultimate "multi-game" machine, offering everything from standard Video Poker to Keno and Blackjack. For the professional grinder, the versatility is a draw, but it also makes **Bankroll Management** more complex.

### Managing Multiple Games
The biggest challenge of Super Star Poker is that you might be switching between games with vastly different **Theoretical Returns** and **Variances**. To achieve **Session Sustainability**, you must have a bankroll that can handle the highest-variance game you plan to play (usually a bonus video poker variant or Keno).

### Variance Buffering
A bankroll that is sufficient for Jacks or Better will be quickly depleted if you switch to a high-variance game like Super Double Double Bonus. We recommend a **Variance Buffering** of at least 800 to 1,000 units if you plan to utilize the full range of games on a Super Star machine.

### "Is Max Bet Always Better for Bankroll Longevity?"
On a Super Star Poker machine, the answer is almost always yes. Whether you are playing poker or one of the other games, the highest payouts and best RTPs are usually locked behind the max bet button. If you are a beginner looking for **video poker tips for beginners**, our best advice for these multi-game machines is to find a game you like and stick to the max bet for that specific game.

### Hit Frequency Optimization
Each game on the Super Star machine has its own **Hit Frequency Optimization**. Switching games too frequently can lead to strategy errors as you fail to adjust your "hold" logic. To learn **how to win video poker daily**, pick one game on the machine and master it before moving on to another.

### RNG and Fairness
Because Super Star Poker is a "software-heavy" machine, some players worry that the **Pseudo-Randomness** is shared across all games. In reality, while they might use the same RNG processor, the math for each game remains independent. A win in Keno does not "use up" your luck for Video Poker.

Super Star Poker offers endless variety, but without a disciplined bankroll strategy, that variety will only lead to a faster bust. Stay focused, stay disciplined, and manage your units wisely.
