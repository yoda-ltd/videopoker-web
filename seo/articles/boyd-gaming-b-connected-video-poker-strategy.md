## The Mechanics of Boyd Rewards for Video Poker

Boyd Rewards is a "tier-based" program with three primary levels: **Ruby, Sapphire, Emerald, Onyx, and Titanium**.

### 1. Points and Tier Credits
*   **Points:** You earn points based on your "coin-in" on most machines. 1,000 points = $1.00 in "Fast Cash" or "Slot Play." 
*   **Tier Credits (TC):** You earn TCs through play and spending. In 2026, the TC earn rate is roughly $5.00 coin-in = 1 TC on most video poker machines. 

### 2. High-Volume Strategy for Tier Levels
*   **Sapphire and Emerald:** These are the most valuable tiers for the mid-stakes grinder. At Emerald status, you get priority check-in and access to the "Emerald Lounges" at Boyd properties across the US.
*   **Multiplier Days:** Boyd is famous for their "Point Multipliers" (e.g., 5x or 10x points) on special days. This is the only way to reach **Onyx** or **Titanium** status without a massive bankroll.
