# Super Double Bonus vs. Royal Flush Bonus: Which Has the Better House Edge?

Comparing **Super Double Bonus** and **Royal Flush Bonus** is a comparison of two ways to target the top of the pay table. While Super Double Bonus rewards you for hitting high-ranking Four-of-a-Kind combinations, Royal Flush Bonus focuses on maximizing the return for the rarest hand in poker. To choose the right game, you must understand how **video poker fairness and regulation** ensures the integrity of these specialized "Jackpot-Centric" models.

## The Mathematical Framework: Quads vs. Royals

The house edge in these games is determined by the "Concentration of Value."

*   **Super Double Bonus:** Value is concentrated in the 4-of-a-kind hands (Aces pay 160-for-1). The edge comes from the player's ability to prioritize specific ranks in their strategic holds.
*   **Royal Flush Bonus:** Value is heavily concentrated in the Royal Flush hit (800-for-1+). The edge comes from the player's ability to maximize their "Royal EV" through aggressive holds, often breaking high-paying hands to chase the jackpot.
