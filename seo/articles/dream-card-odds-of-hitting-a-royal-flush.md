### Probability Shift

*   This effectively turns a 1 in 47 draw into a **100% certainty** for the Royal Flush.

Because of this, the **Royal Flush Cycle** in Dream Card (when the feature is active) is significantly shorter—dropping from 1 in 40,000 hands to approximately 1 in 25,000 hands.
