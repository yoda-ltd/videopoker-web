### Strategic Divergence: Bonus Quads vs. Card Totals

In **Double Bonus Poker**, your return is entirely dependent on your ability to make the mathematically correct hold decisions, specifically focusing on the high-payout four-of-a-kind hands. This is a game of **Volatility Normalization** through disciplined play. In Double Bonus, you are holding cards for bonus quads; in Blackjack, you are hitting or standing for 21; in Craps, you are placing chips for dice results.

### Key Strategy Adjustments
1.  **Double Bonus Skill:** You must master the exact strategy for bonus quads (Aces, 2s-4s, 5s-Ks).
2.  **Blackjack Discipline:** You must follow Basic Strategy perfectly to achieve the 0.50% house edge.
3.  **Craps Simplicity:** To achieve the best odds in Craps, you simply place the Pass Line bet and back it with the maximum allowed "Odds" bet. This is a form of **Payout Compression**—the "Odds" bet has zero house edge.

Through a detailed **video poker house edge analysis**, it's clear that Double Bonus offers a superior return compared to standard Blackjack and Craps, provided you are willing to master the complex strategy. However, Blackjack offers a much lower variance, making it a smoother ride for the risk-averse player.
