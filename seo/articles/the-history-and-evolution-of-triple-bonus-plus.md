## The Origin: The Late 1990s Payout Shift
The **history of Triple Bonus Poker Plus video poker** begins in **1998-1999**. At the time, IGT’s *Double Bonus Poker* was a massive hit, but players were becoming accustomed to the "Double" formula. 

**International Game Technology (IGT)** and other manufacturers like **Action Gaming** (the pioneers of Multi-Play) introduced **Triple Bonus Poker Plus** as a high-volatility variant of *Double Bonus*. The **Digital Logic Transition** was elegant: 
*   **Four Aces:** Paid 240-for-1 (1,200 coins on a 5-coin bet).
*   **Four 2s, 3s, or 4s:** Paid 120-for-1.
*   **Four 5s through Kings:** Paid 50-for-1.

This shift in **Pay Table Evolution** was a brilliant move by IGT. It gave players a massive jackpot feel for the "Bonus" hands, not just the rare Royal Flush.

## Why Triple Bonus Poker Plus Became "Legendary"
While variants like *Double Double Bonus* offer even higher volatility, Triple Bonus Poker Plus became a legend for its:
1.  **Simplicity:** No need to track kickers or different quads. If you hit four Aces, you get 240-for-1. This simplicity made it a favorite for recreational players who wanted big wins without complex rules.
2.  **High RTP:** The "Full Pay" version of Triple Bonus Poker Plus (often called 9/5) offers an RTP of **99.58%** with perfect strategy. This makes it one of the best-returning games in the casino, even better than 9/6 Jacks or Better (99.54%) or 8/5 Bonus Poker (99.17%).
3.  **Strategy Shift:** The **evolution of video poker games** saw Triple Bonus Poker Plus develop a strategy that was very different from Jacks or Better. Because four Aces pay 240-for-1, players are much more likely to hold any Ace, even a lone one, which would be a mistake in almost any other game.

## SEO and the Evolution of Video Poker
When discussing the **evolution of video poker games**, Triple Bonus Poker Plus is often cited as the first "uniform bonus" variant. It proved that a simple, high-impact payout table could create a loyal following. This **Patent History** of uniform payout modification would lead to modern games like *Triple Double Bonus* and *Super Star Poker*.
