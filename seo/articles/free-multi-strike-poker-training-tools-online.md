Multi-Strike Poker is a specialized video poker variant that requires a unique strategy to master. ## Why Training Tools are Essential

### Mastery of "Survival Play" (Levels 1 & 2)
On Level 1 (1x multiplier) and Level 2 (2x multiplier), your goal is **Survival**. Any winning hand (even a low pair) guarantees advancement to the next level. 
- **The Correct Play**: Prioritize high-probability winning hands at the bottom of the ladder to reach the 8x multiplier on Level 4.
- **The Pitfall**: Chasing a low-probability, high-payout hand on Level 1.

### "Free Ride" and "Bonus Streak" Adjustments
The "Free Ride" mechanic is a critical feature in Multi-Strike. When active, you advance to the next level regardless of the hand's outcome. 
- **Strategy Shift**: When a Free Ride is active, your **strategy shifts** to aggressive "Jackpot Chasing." 
- **Bonus Streak Tips**: Many Multi-Strike machines feature "Bonus Streak" payouts for consecutive wins. Using **bonus streak video poker tips** to maintain these multipliers can lead to a much higher **Bonus Trigger Frequency**.
