# The History and Evolution of Bonus Streak Poker

Bonus Streak Poker is a "Feature-Driven" variant that represents the era of "Interactive" video poker. It moved away from the "One Hand at a Time" mentality and introduced a "Streak" mechanic that rewarded players for winning consecutive hands.

## The "Streak" Concept

In the mid-2000s, video poker was competing for attention on floors filled with "Multi-Level Progressive" slots. Developers realized that the "Thrill of a Winning Streak" was a powerful psychological hook. Bonus Streak Poker was designed to capture this feeling.

By placing a "Bonus Bet" (usually 10 coins instead of 5), players activated the "Streak" feature. If you hit a Three of a Kind or better, you started a "Streak," and subsequent wins on that "line" would receive a multiplier.

## Evolutionary Milestones

1.  **The "Consecutive Win" Mechanic:** This was the first major video poker game to link the result of one hand to the *payout* of the next. It changed the "Independent Trial" nature of the game (at least in terms of rewards).
2.  **Visual "Thermometers":** Bonus Streak machines featured "Streak Meters" or "Thermometers" on the side of the screen, showing players how close they were to reaching a 5x or 10x multiplier.
3.  **The "Multi-Hand" Boom:** Bonus Streak Poker reached its peak popularity on "Triple Play" and "Five Play" machines, where players could manage multiple "Streaks" simultaneously, creating a complex, high-energy experience.

## Video Poker Terms Every Player Should Know

When searching the complete video poker glossary, these terms are vital for Bonus Streak:

*   **Action:** The "Bonus Bet" effectively doubles your coin-in per hour, leading to faster "Action" and more casino rewards (comps).
*   **Variance:** This is a "Very High" variance game. The "Streak" feature can lead to massive payouts, but you are paying a "tax" on every hand to keep the feature active.
*   **Expected Value (EV):** To achieve a high EV in Bonus Streak, you must play with a "Streak-Aware" strategy, which is significantly more complex than standard poker strategy.

## Strategy and "Momentum"

The evolution of Bonus Streak Poker introduced the concept of "Momentum" to the game. Players felt a sense of "Investment" in a machine if they were currently on a 3x multiplier, making it much harder to walk away—a psychological effect that casinos leveraged to increase "Time on Device."

## Legacy of the "Streak"

Bonus Streak Poker paved the way for modern "Accumulator" games where the machine "remembers" your progress (like *Scarabs* or *Ocean Magic* slots, but for poker). It proved that video poker players enjoyed a "Quest-style" experience where they were working toward a specific goal.

Today, while original Bonus Streak cabinets are harder to find, the "Multiplier Streak" mechanic has been refined and included in many "Ultimate X" and "Super Times Pay" variants. It remains a fascinating chapter in the "Complete Video Poker Glossary" of feature-rich gaming.
