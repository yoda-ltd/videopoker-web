# Common Mistakes to Avoid in Aces and Faces

Aces and Faces is a popular video poker variant that offers bonus payouts for Four-of-a-Kind hands involving Aces or Face cards. While the **Theoretical Return** can be high, many players sabotage their chances by making common errors.

### Chasing the Bonus
The biggest mistake in Aces and Faces is overvaluing the bonus hands. Players often break apart a winning hand (like a Full House) to chase a Four-of-a-Kind in Aces. This destroys your **Hit Frequency Optimization**. Unless the strategy chart explicitly says to break a hand, you should keep your guaranteed wins to maintain **Session Sustainability**.

### Ignoring the Pay Table
Not all Aces and Faces machines are created equal. You might find an 8/5 machine when a 9/6 or 7/5 machine is also available. Even a 1-unit difference in the payout for a Full House or Flush can significantly lower the RTP. For those asking **how to win video poker daily**, the answer starts with machine selection.

### Betting Less Than Max
"Is max bet always better for bankroll longevity?" In Aces and Faces, the answer is yes. The Royal Flush bonus is only active at max bet. If you are a beginner looking for **video poker tips for beginners**, the first thing you should learn is that playing fewer than 5 coins is effectively giving the casino a 1.5% gift.

### Misunderstanding the RNG
Some players think that if they just missed a Four-of-a-Kind, the machine is "due" to hit one soon. This is a misunderstanding of **Pseudo-Randomness vs. True Randomness**. Every deal is independent. The machine has no memory of the "near miss" you just experienced.

### Bankroll Mismanagement
Because Aces and Faces has bonus payouts, the **Variance** is slightly higher than in Jacks or Better. You need a larger bankroll to handle the swings. If you don't have enough **Variance Buffering**, a cold streak will end your session early.

Avoid these mistakes, stick to a proven strategy, and you'll find that Aces and Faces can be one of the most rewarding games on the casino floor.
