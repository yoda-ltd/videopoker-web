# Bankroll Management for Super Aces Bonus Grinders

Super Aces Bonus is one of the most volatile games in the video poker world. With massive payouts for Four-of-a-Kind Aces, the game is a "thrill ride" that requires a disciplined approach to **Bankroll Management**.

### The Volatility Factor
Super Aces Bonus has a **Theoretical Return** that can be very high, but it is extremely "top-heavy." You will spend most of your time losing small amounts while waiting for those Four Aces to appear. To achieve **Session Sustainability**, you need a bankroll of at least 1,500 to 2,500 units. Without this level of **Variance Buffering**, the odds of you busting before hitting a big hand are very high.

### "Is Max Bet Always Better for Bankroll Longevity?"
In Super Aces, the 5-coin bet is non-negotiable. The massive bonus for the Aces is almost always tied to the maximum bet. If you play for 1 coin, you are playing a game with a significantly lower RTP and much higher house edge. For those asking **how to win video poker daily**, the first rule is: never play Super Aces for less than 5 coins.

### Hit Frequency Optimization
Because the bonus for Aces is so high, the strategy for Super Aces involves holding a single Ace more aggressively than in other games. However, you still need to balance this with **Hit Frequency Optimization** for other hands to keep your bankroll afloat. If you are a beginner looking for **video poker tips for beginners**, follow a Super Aces-specific strategy chart to avoid over-chasing the Aces.

### The RNG Reality
The "near miss" of three Aces is common in this game, leading many to believe the machine is teasing them. This is simply the result of **Pseudo-Randomness**. The RNG doesn't have a "tease" mode; it simply deals the cards. Every hand is an independent event with a 1/52 chance for any specific card to appear.

### Daily Success
To succeed as a Super Aces grinder, you must have the stomach for the swings. Set a strict loss limit for the day and stick to it. If you hit the Four Aces early, consider walking away with your profit.

Super Aces is the ultimate game for the "Aces-hunter," but only if you have the bankroll to support the hunt.
