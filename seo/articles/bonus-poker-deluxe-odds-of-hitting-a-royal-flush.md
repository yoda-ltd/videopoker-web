# Bonus Poker Deluxe: Odds of Hitting a Royal Flush

The Royal Flush is the crown jewel of any video poker game. In **Bonus Poker Deluxe**, the thrill of chasing this elusive hand is combined with a pay table that rewards Quads (Four of a Kind) more generously than standard Jacks or Better. But what are the actual mathematical odds of hitting a Royal Flush in Bonus Poker Deluxe, and how should they influence your strategy?

## The Mathematical Probability

In a standard 52-card deck, there are **2,598,960** possible five-card hands. Of these, exactly **4** are Royal Flushes (one for each suit).

When playing with optimal strategy, the odds of hitting a Royal Flush in Bonus Poker Deluxe are approximately **1 in 40,000 to 1 in 45,000 hands**. 

The variance in these odds depends on the specific pay table and how strictly you follow the "Royal Flush-centric" strategy. Because Bonus Poker Deluxe pays 80-for-1 on all Four of a Kind hands, the strategy is slightly more aggressive toward holding pairs, but the Royal Flush remains the primary target for the top payout.

### Breakdown of Royal Flush Odds
- **Initial Deal:** The probability of being dealt a Royal Flush on the first five cards is **1 in 649,740**.
- **After the Draw:** Most Royal Flushes are completed after the draw. If you are dealt 4 cards to a Royal, your odds of hitting the fifth card are **1 in 47** (since 47 cards remain in the deck).

## How Pay Tables Affect the "Chase"

Bonus Poker Deluxe is unique because it offers a flat 400-coin payout (on a 5-coin bet) for *any* Four of a Kind. This is a significant bump from Jacks or Better. However, the Royal Flush remains the jackpot, paying **4,000 coins** on a max-bet (5 coins).

| Hand | Payout (Max Bet) |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Royal Flush** | **4,000** |
| Four of a Kind (Any) | 400 |
| Straight Flush | 250 |
| Full House (9/6 Version) | 45 |

### The "Max Bet" Rule
To achieve the 800-for-1 odds (4,000 coins) on a Royal Flush, you **must** bet the maximum of 5 coins. Betting 1-4 coins typically only pays 250-for-1 for a Royal, which significantly increases the house edge and ruins your long-term expected value (EV).

## Strategic Tips for Hitting the Royal

To maximize your chances of hitting the Royal Flush in Bonus Poker Deluxe without sacrificing too much overall return:

1.  **Never Break a Pat Royal:** This goes without saying, but always hold all five cards of a dealt Royal.
2.  **Hold 4 to a Royal over a Straight or Flush:** Even if you have a guaranteed payout with a made Straight or Flush, the math dictates you should break it to go for the Royal Flush if you have 4 cards toward it.
3.  **Hold 3 to a Royal over a Low Pair:** In most Bonus Poker Deluxe variations, three cards to a Royal Flush have a higher expected value than a low pair (2s through 10s).
4.  **Hold 2 to a Royal (High Cards):** If you have two high cards of the same suit (e.g., King and Queen of Hearts), they are often worth keeping over a single high card or a "trash" hand.

## Summary

While the **1 in 40,000** odds might seem daunting, it is important to remember that Video Poker is a game of the long run. Bonus Poker Deluxe offers a smoother ride than high-variance games like Triple Double Bonus, thanks to its frequent 80-for-1 Quad payouts. However, the Royal Flush is what pushes the game toward its maximum theoretical return of **99.64%** (on the 9/6 version). 

Always play max coins, follow a verified strategy chart, and enjoy the hunt for those five magical cards.
