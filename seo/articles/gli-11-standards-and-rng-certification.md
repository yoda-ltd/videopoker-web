# The Global Standard for Video Poker Fairness: Understanding GLI-11

In the competitive landscape of online and land-based gambling, **video poker fairness and regulation** are the pillars that uphold player trust. How do we know that the "Random" in Random Number Generator is actually random? The answer lies in the **GLI-11 Standards**, the industry's most rigorous framework established by **Gaming Laboratories International (GLI)**.

## What is GLI-11?
GLI-11 is a technical standard officially titled "Standards for Gaming Devices in Casinos." It serves as the definitive guide for how gaming software and hardware must function to be considered fair, secure, and auditable. While other labs like **BMM Testlabs** have their own frameworks, GLI-11 is often cited as the gold standard for **Jurisdictional Mandates**.

### 1. The Random Number Generator (RNG) Requirement
The core of GLI-11 is the **trusted video poker RNG certification**. The standards mandate that an RNG must:
*   **Be statistically independent:** Previous outcomes must have zero influence on future deals.
*   **Conform to a specific distribution:** In video poker, this means an exactly 1 in 52 chance for any specific card to be drawn from a virtual deck.
*   **Pass tests for "Unpredictability":** Even with sophisticated software, a player should never be able to predict the next card in the sequence.

In professional video poker environments, this means the software must constantly "shuffle" the deck, even when no one is playing, to ensure maximum entropy.

### 2. Return to Player (RTP) Verification
GLI-11 requires that the payout table shown on the screen must be achievable through the game's mathematical logic. This involves a rigorous **Return to Player (RTP) Verification** process. If a machine says it pays 800 for a Royal Flush, **Lab Audits** must prove that a Royal Flush occurs at the mathematically correct frequency based on a random 52-card deck.

## Global Regulatory Alignment
Different regions use GLI-11 as the basis for their own local laws.
- **Nevada (NGCB):** Uses similar technical standards to ensure Las Vegas machines are fair.
- **UK (UKGC):** Mandates that all UK-facing operators provide proof of certification against standards equivalent to GLI-11.
- **Michigan (MGCB):** Requires online operators to submit software for testing against these standards before going live.

## The Transition: From Physical Audits to API Integration
The process of maintaining **game integrity** has evolved significantly:
*   **Digital Hash Verification:** Regulators use digital signatures to ensure the software on the casino floor matches the version certified in the lab.
*   **Continuous Monitoring:** Modern systems allow labs to perform periodic re-certification to ensure no unauthorized changes have been made to the payout logic.

## Conclusion: The Foundation of Game Integrity
Trust is the most important currency in gambling. Without the rigorous **Technical Standards** set by GLI-11, the global video poker industry would lack the transparency required for high-stakes play. When you see a GLI or BMM seal on a gaming site, you know that the **RNG Certification** and **RTP Verification** have been handled by the world's leading experts in game integrity.
