# The History and Evolution of Double Jackpot Poker

Double Jackpot Poker stands as a testament to the "Bonus" era of video poker, where players began demanding higher payouts for specific "Four of a Kind" combinations. This article tracks the history and evolution of this high-octane variant.

## The Rise of the Bonus Hand

In the mid-1990s, the video poker landscape shifted. While Jacks or Better provided steady play, it lacked the "big hit" potential that slot players enjoyed. Double Jackpot Poker was developed to bridge this gap by offering "Jackpot" payouts for specific quads, particularly Aces and face cards.

## Key Evolutionary Milestones

1.  **Pay Table Diversification:** Unlike standard games, Double Jackpot Poker introduced a tiered payout system for Four of a Kind. Aces with a specific kicker (usually a face card) paid significantly more than other quads.
2.  **The "Kicker" Mechanic:** This game helped popularize the importance of the fifth card (the kicker) in determining the payout of a Four of a Kind hand, a mechanic that would later be perfected in Double Double Bonus.
3.  **High Stakes Appeal:** The "Double Jackpot" branding was a marketing masterstroke, signaling to players that this machine was designed for those chasing large, lumpy payouts rather than small, frequent wins.

## Professional Terminology in Double Jackpot

When discussing Double Jackpot Poker, several video poker terms every player should know come into play:

*   **Bankroll:** Because of the high payouts on specific quads, the variance is high, requiring a larger bankroll than standard games.
*   **Full Pay:** A "Full Pay" Double Jackpot machine is rare but highly sought after by professionals for its near-100% return with perfect strategy.
*   **Cycle:** The cycle for the "Double Jackpot" hands (Aces with a Kicker) is much longer than a standard Four of a Kind, making it a "big game hunter's" variant.

## Impact on the Complete Video Poker Glossary

Double Jackpot Poker helped define several entries in the complete video poker glossary, specifically regarding "Bonus Poker" and "Kicker" definitions. It proved that players were willing to accept lower payouts on smaller hands (like two pair) in exchange for the thrill of a "Jackpot" quad.

## Double Jackpot Poker Today

Today, Double Jackpot Poker is a staple on IGT Game King machines. It remains a favorite for players who find Jacks or Better too slow but aren't quite ready for the extreme volatility of Triple Double Bonus. Its evolution from a niche variant to a casino floor standard highlights the player's eternal desire for the "Big Hit."
