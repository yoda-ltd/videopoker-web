# Bankroll Management for Dice Poker Grinders
Category: Strategy

Bankroll management for Dice Poker is a breath of fresh air for the seasoned video poker player. Because the game has only 7,776 total combinations (compared to over 2.5 million in card poker), the "Hit Frequency" is extremely high. This means your bankroll is far more stable, and the "Variance" is significantly lower. However, because the top jackpots are smaller (usually 150:1), you must manage your funds differently to ensure long-term profitability.

In this guide, we will discuss the "Micro-Bankroll" strategy and how to capitalize on the high hit frequency of Dice Poker.

## The "Micro-Bankroll" Advantage

In Dice Poker, you "Win" something on nearly 45-50% of your hands. This creates a very "Flat" bankroll curve.
- **The Unit**: 1 Unit = Total bet for one hand (usually 5 coins).

## 1. The "Single Session" Bankroll
Because the game is so stable, you can play a full 4-hour session on a very small amount of capital.
- **Recommendation**: **200 Units.**
- **Quarter Player**: $250.
- **Dollar Player**: $1,000.

In 4 hours of play (~2,000 hands), you will likely hit multiple "Five of a Kind" (150:1) or "Four of a Kind" (25:1) hands. These frequent payouts prevent the "Deep Dives" that occur in card poker.

## 2. The "Long-Term" Professional Bankroll
Even for a professional Dice Poker grinder, the total capital required is relatively low.
- **Recommendation**: **1,000 Units.**
- **Quarter Player**: $1,250.
- **Dollar Player**: $5,000.

This bankroll is sufficient to handle any statistical outlier. It is virtually impossible to go "Bust" on a 1,000-unit bankroll in Dice Poker if you are playing perfect strategy on a competitive machine.

## Key Strategies for the Dice Poker Grinder

### A. The "Volume over Jackpot" Rule
In card poker, you are playing for the 800:1 Royal Flush. In Dice Poker, you are playing for the **150:1 Five of a Kind.**
- **Strategy**: Because the top prize is smaller, your hourly ROI is lower. To make a "Living" at Dice Poker, you must play at an extremely high volume (1,200+ hands per hour).
- **Bankroll Tip**: Since you are playing so fast, ensure your machine has a "Turbo" or "Fast Deal" setting to maximize your "Hands per Hour."

### B. Leveraging the "Small Straight"
Many Dice Poker machines pay for "Small Straights" (4 in a row). This is your "Stable Income." 
- **Bankroll Tip**: Treat the Small Straight as your "Salary." It keeps your bankroll level while you wait for the "Five of a Kind" bonus. If the machine you are playing doesn't pay for Small Straights, you need a larger bankroll.

### C. The Comp Engine
Because Dice Poker is so stable, you can "Churn" a massive amount of coin-in with very little risk to your principal. 
- **The Pro Edge**: The "Profit" in Dice Poker often comes 100% from the casino comps. If you can play 10,000 hands and only lose $50 in "Theoretical House Edge," but the casino gives you $100 in free play and meals, you are a professional winner.

## Summary Checklist
1. **Enjoy the Low Variance**: Don't be afraid to play at a higher denomination than you would in card poker.
2. **Maximize Your Speed**: High volume is the only way to make the 150:1 jackpot pay off.
3. **Verify the "Five of a Kind" Payout**: Ensure it's at least 150:1. If it's 100:1, the game is not worth your time.
4. **Bank the Jackpots**: When you hit the 150:1, move it to your "Profit" fund immediately.

Dice Poker is the "Safe Haven" of the gambling world. By following a disciplined "Micro-Bankroll" strategy and focusing on high-volume play, you can enjoy a stable, professional-grade gambling experience with minimal risk.
