# The History and Evolution of Super Aces Bonus Poker

Super Aces Bonus Poker is a high-intensity variant of video poker that traces its roots back to the "Aces and Faces" and "Bonus Poker" crazes of the 1990s. It represents the pinnacle of "Ace-chasing" strategy, where the Four of a Kind Aces payout is the central focus of the game.

## Origins: The Quest for the "Super" Payout

As video poker evolved, developers realized that players had a psychological affinity for Aces. While a standard Royal Flush is the ultimate goal, it is extremely rare. Developers created "Bonus" games to provide a more frequent, yet still substantial, "mid-range" jackpot.

Super Aces was the natural evolution of "Double Bonus" and "Double Double Bonus." It took the payout for Four Aces and pushed it to its logical extreme—often paying 2,000 coins (at a 5-coin bet), which is exactly half of a Royal Flush payout.

## Evolution of the Strategy

The introduction of Super Aces changed how professionals approached the game:

1.  **Aggressive Discarding:** In Super Aces, the value of Aces is so high that players will often break up "winning" hands (like a Full House or two pair) just for a chance at drawing more Aces.
2.  **Increased Volatility:** By shifting so much of the prize pool into a single hand (Four Aces), the game became much "swingier" than Jacks or Better.
3.  **The "Kicker" Factor:** Unlike Double Double Bonus, Super Aces typically doesn't require a specific kicker for the top Ace payout, making the "Super" win slightly easier to hit, though the rest of the pay table is usually "tighter" to compensate.

## Professional Terminology for Super Aces

Here are video poker terms every player should know when playing Super Aces:

*   **Action:** High-volume players generate massive "action" on Super Aces because the high Ace payouts keep them in the game longer during "hot" streaks.
*   **Bankroll:** You need a "Deep Bankroll" for Super Aces. The variance is extreme, and you can lose hundreds of credits quickly if the Aces don't appear.
*   **Expected Value (EV):** Strategy for Super Aces is entirely built around maximizing the EV of the Ace-related combinations.

## Impact on the Complete Video Poker Glossary

Super Aces helped popularize terms like "Ace-Heavy Pay Table" and "Quad-centric Strategy." It remains a top choice for players who want the excitement of a Royal Flush-style win without waiting for the 40,000-to-1 odds of a natural Royal.

## Modern Status

Super Aces Bonus Poker is now a standard feature on most "Triple Play" and "Five Play" multi-hand machines. Its evolution from a standalone cabinet to a multi-hand powerhouse shows its enduring popularity among players who live by the motto: "Aces are everything."
