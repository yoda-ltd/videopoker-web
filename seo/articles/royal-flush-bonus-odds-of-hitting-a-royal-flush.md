## The Mathematical Foundation of the Royal Flush

The natural probability of being dealt a Royal Flush in a standard 52-card deck is exactly 1 in 649,740. However, video poker is a game of draw. In a typical Jacks or Better variant, with perfect strategy, you can expect to hit a Royal Flush approximately once every 40,000 hands. 

In **Royal Flush Bonus**, the "Cycle" is your primary metric. The **Royal Flush Cycle** represents the average number of hands required to hit the top prize. Depending on the specific pay table (9/6 vs 8/5), this number usually fluctuates between 40,000 and 43,000 hands.

## Chasing the Jackpot: When to Break Winning Hands

One of the most complex aspects of **progressive video poker strategy** is knowing when to deviate from standard "safe" play to chase the Royal. This is where **Mathematical Convergence** comes into play.

### Breaking a Flush or Straight
If you are dealt four cards to a Royal Flush but already have a made Flush or Straight, the math almost always dictates breaking the winning hand to chase the Royal. The Expected Value (EV) of a 4-card Royal (approximately 18.3 to 19.5 units depending on the jackpot) far exceeds the 4 or 6 units offered by a Straight or Flush.

### The Full House Dilemma: Breaking for a 3-Card Royal?
This is where professional gambling terminology like **Progressive Reset EV** becomes vital. Normally, you would never break a Full House. However, if the progressive jackpot has climbed to a level where the **Break-even Meter** is surpassed, the EV of a 3-card Royal Flush might actually challenge the 9-unit payout of the Full House.

## The Break-Even Meter: Achieving 100%+ RTP

A cornerstone of **progressive video poker strategy** is identifying the point at which the game reaches a mathematical player advantage (positive EV).

Every progressive machine has a "Reset Value" (usually 4,000 coins for a 5-coin bet). At this reset, the RTP (Return to Player) might be 99.54%. As the meter rises, so does the RTP. For a standard 9/6 Jacks or Better progressive, the game reaches **100% RTP** when the Royal Flush jackpot hits approximately **2,158 credits** (on a 1-coin basis, or 10,790 on a 5-coin bet).
