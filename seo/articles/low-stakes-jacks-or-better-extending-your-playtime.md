## Strategy: The "9/6" Standard

The strategy for Jacks or Better is the most well-documented in the gambling world. 
1. **The Low Pair Hold:** In Jacks or Better, holding a low pair is almost always the correct move over a single high card. This is the bedrock of the "low variance" experience.

3. **Low Variance:** Because the payouts are spread more evenly across the paytable (from a pair of Jacks to the Royal Flush), you won't experience the massive "dry spells" of a slot machine.
