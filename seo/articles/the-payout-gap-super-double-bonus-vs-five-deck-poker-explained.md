# The Payout Gap: Super Double Bonus vs. Five-Deck Poker Explained
Category: Comparison

Understanding the **Strategic Divergence** between **Super Double Bonus** and **Five-Deck Poker** is key to mastering these high-action variants. This comparison focuses on how the paytables differ and why those differences lead to unique **EV Maximization** paths.

### The Comparison Matrix: Super Double Bonus vs. Five-Deck Poker

| Feature | Super Double Bonus (10/7) | Five-Deck Poker (Full Pay) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **RTP (Theoretical)** | 100.17% (Full Pay) | 99.40% |
| **Variance Index** | 28.5 | 20.0 |
| **Primary Payout Focus** | High-Payout Natural Quads | Multi-Deck Connectivity (5 Decks) |
| **House Edge Analysis** | -0.17% (Player Edge) | 0.60% |

### The Connectivity Gap: 5 Decks vs. High-Payout Quads

In **Five-Deck Poker**, the reward is concentrated in Achieving high-payout hands across five independent decks. In **Super Double Bonus Poker**, the reward is concentrated in Achieving natural four-of-a-kind hands that pay a massive bonus. This creates a significant **Volatility Normalization** shift. In Five-Deck Poker, you focus on Natural connectivity across all five decks, while in Super Double Bonus, you prioritize high-bonus natural quads.

**Strategy Overlap:** There is roughly 60% overlap on the core hand results. However, in Five-Deck Poker, you are looking for Straights and Flushes much more frequently because you have five independent decks to draw from. This is the essence of **Payout Compression**—the connectivity in Five-Deck Poker dominates the EV landscape.

### Strategic Key Shifts
1.  **Connectivity Priority:** In Five-Deck Poker, prioritize 4-to-a-flush and 4-to-a-straight across multiple decks.
2.  **Quad Awareness:** In Super Double Bonus, prioritize natural quads above almost all other hands.
3.  **Return Priority:** Super Double Bonus offers a superior theoretical return (100.17%), making it the better choice for the advantage player seeking an edge.

The **best video poker strategy comparison** indicates that Super Double Bonus is the superior game for the expert player seeking an edge, while Five-Deck Poker is better for those who want a stable ride with unique deck features. A **video poker house edge analysis** confirms that both are elite choices for those who master their respective strategies.

### The Ace Gap: Broad vs. Focused Natural Bonuses

### Strategic Key Shifts

2.  **Broad Quad Focus:** In Super Double Bonus, prioritize natural quads across all tiered ranks.
3.  **Return Priority:** Super Double Bonus offers a superior theoretical return (100.17%), making it the better choice for the expert player seeking an edge.

Through a detailed **video poker house edge analysis**, it's clear that while Super Double Bonus offers the best return, Triple Aces is the more straightforward choice for those who want a simple, natural game. The **best video poker strategy comparison** for this transition is: Use the broad bonuses for the edge, or the Aces for the jackpot.
