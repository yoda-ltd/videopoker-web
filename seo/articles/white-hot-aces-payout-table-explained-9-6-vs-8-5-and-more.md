## The Best Odds Index: 9/6 White Hot Aces (99.57%)
In the **highest return video poker games** 2026 index, 9/6 White Hot Aces is a top-tier choice. It offers a **Theoretical Return Convergence of 99.57%**.

The "9/6" designation here refers to:
*   **Full House:** 9 coins per 1 coin bet.
*   **Flush:** 6 coins per 1 coin bet.

Wait, why do we call it "9/6" White Hot Aces? It's the pay table that pays **9 coins for a Full House AND 6 coins for a Flush**.

## Analysis of "Short Pay" Predation
Because 99.57% gives the player a strong return, casinos often use "Short Pay" tables to reverse it.
*   **8/5 White Hot Aces:** Paying only 8 for Full House and 5 for Flush drops the RTP to **97.33%**.
*   **7/5 White Hot Aces:** Paying 7 for Full House and 5 for Flush drops the RTP to **96.22%**.
*   **Common Short Pay (8/4):** This variant drops the RTP below **97.37%**.

The **EV Gap** between the 99.57% version and the 96.22% version is over 3.35%. If you are playing 800 hands per hour at a $1 machine, that’s $33.5 per hour you’re losing simply by picking the wrong machine. This "predatory" short-pay table kills the professional edge.
