## Instant Dice vs. Delayed Keno
The core difference lies in the timing and nature of the bonus.

- **Hot Roll Poker (The Instant Multiplier):** On the deal, a pair of dice rolls and immediately applies a 2x-12x multiplier to any winning hand. The result is instant.
- **Keno Poker (The Multi-Stage Bonus):** You play a standard hand of video poker, but certain winning hands (like Three-of-a-Kind) trigger a "Keno Draw." A set of Keno numbers appears, and if they match your pre-selected spots, you win a massive jackpot.

## Key Strategic Adjustments

### 1. From "Pure Card EV" to "Feature-Hunting"
- **In Hot Roll Poker:** The multiplier applies to *every* win. Therefore, you should stick to standard "Pure EV" (Jacks or Better or DDB) to maximize the likelihood of *any* win.
- **In Keno Poker:** You may actually *deviate* from standard strategy to hit the hands that trigger the Keno draw. If Three-of-a-Kind triggers the Keno draw but a High Pair does not, the value of those trips increases dramatically. You are playing for the "Keno Event," not just the poker hand.

### 2. Variance Normalization and Bankroll Management
- **Hot Roll Poker Shift:** Extreme variance. The 10-coin bet is constant, but the 12x multiplier is rare. Your bankroll will move in jagged, violent lines.
- **Keno Poker Shift:** "Hybrid Variance." You have the steady wins of video poker mixed with the "Lottery-Style" volatility of Keno. 
- **The Adjustment:** Keno Poker requires more patience. You aren't getting a multiplier every 6 hands; you are waiting for the specific poker hand that triggers the Keno "Stage." You need a bankroll that can survive the long gaps between Keno events.

### 3. Feature Hit Frequency
- **Hot Roll Poker:** High frequency (Dice roll ~16% of hands).
- **Keno Poker:** Low frequency (Keno draw ~2-5% of hands depending on the version).
- **The Shift:** In Hot Roll, the "Feature" is part of the rhythm of the game. In Keno Poker, the "Feature" is a "Special Event." Moving to Keno Poker requires a shift from "Action" to "Anticipation."
