# The History and Evolution of Five Deck Poker

Five Deck Poker is a fascinating "anomaly" in the history of video poker. While 99% of video poker games use a standard 52-card deck (or 53 with a Joker), Five Deck Poker introduced a radical new way to shuffle the virtual deck, changing the mathematics of the game forever.

## The "Multi-Deck" Experiment

In the late 1990s, as casinos were looking for ways to refresh the video poker floor, some developers took inspiration from Blackjack. In Blackjack, using multiple decks increases the house edge and changes the strategy. Five Deck Poker applied this to the poker machine.

By drawing from five separate 52-card decks, the game made it possible to hit hands that are physically impossible in standard poker—such as **Five of a Kind (without a wild card)** or even a "Five-Ace" hand.

## How the Game Evolved

1.  **New Winning Combinations:** The primary evolution was the creation of a new "top tier" of hands. The payout for a "Natural Five of a Kind" (all Five Aces, for example) became a legendary "Jackpot" hand.
2.  **Probability Shift:** In a 52-card deck, your chance of drawing a specific card (like the King of Hearts) decreases as other cards are dealt. In Five Deck Poker, the probability remains more stable, as there are four other Kings of Hearts still in the "shuffler."
3.  **Visual Innovation:** These machines used custom graphics to show which "deck" each card was coming from, ensuring players understood that the "Duplicate" cards were not a glitch but a feature.

## Essential Video Poker Terminology

For players exploring this multi-deck world, these are video poker terms every player should know:

*   **RNG (Random Number Generator):** The RNG for Five Deck Poker is significantly more complex than a standard machine, as it must simulate the interactions of 260 cards simultaneously.
*   **Variance:** Five Deck Poker has "Ultra-High Variance." The probability of hitting a "Five of a Kind" is low, but the payout is massive, leading to steep "swings" in your bankroll.
*   **Expected Value (EV):** Strategy for Five Deck Poker is entirely different from Jacks or Better. You must consult a specific "Five-Deck Strategy Chart" to maintain a high EV, as the presence of duplicate cards changes the math of every "Hold."

## Impact on the Complete Video Poker Glossary

Five Deck Poker helped define several unique terms in the complete video poker glossary, including "Duplicate Card Bonus" and "Multi-Deck Shuffler." It proved that players were willing to step outside the boundaries of "Traditional Poker" if the rewards were high enough.

## The Legacy of the Five Decks

While Five Deck Poker is a niche game today, its influence can be seen in modern "Slot-Poker Hybrid" games. It remains a cult favorite for players who want to experience the "impossible"—hitting a hand that simply cannot exist in a home poker game. It is a reminder that in the virtual world of video poker, the only limit is the developer's imagination.
