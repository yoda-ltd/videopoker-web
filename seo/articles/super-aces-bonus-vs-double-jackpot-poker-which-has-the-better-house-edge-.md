# Super Aces Bonus vs. Double Jackpot Poker: Which Has the Better House Edge?

Comparing **Super Aces Bonus** and **Double Jackpot Poker** is a study in rank-specific focus. While Super Aces Bonus concentrates its value in hitting Four Aces, Double Jackpot Poker rewards you for high-ranking Quads (Aces, Face Cards) and often includes a bonus for hitting specific 4-of-a-kind combinations with a kicker. To choose the right game, you must understand how **video poker fairness and regulation** ensures the integrity of these specialized "Jackpot-Centric" models.

## The Mathematical Framework: Aces vs. All Faces

The house edge in these games is determined by how the "Big Wins" are distributed.

*   **Super Aces Bonus:** Value is heavily weighted toward hitting Four Aces. The 400-for-1 (2,000 coins at max bet) payout is the primary driver of the RTP.
*   **Double Jackpot Poker:** Value is distributed more broadly across Aces, Kings, Queens, and Jacks. The edge comes from the frequency of hitting any of these high-value Quads, often with a "Kicker" requirement for the top prizes.
