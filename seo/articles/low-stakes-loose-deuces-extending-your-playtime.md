## The Power of the Deuce: Slots vs. Loose Deuces

On a typical slot machine, "Wild" symbols appear randomly. In Loose Deuces, all four 2s (Deuces) in the deck are permanently wild. This changes the entire mathematical landscape of the game.
While a standard "Jacks or Better" paytable might pay 125-for-1 for a Royal Flush, Loose Deuces can offer an **800-for-1 or even 4,000-coin payout** for a "Natural" Royal Flush, while also providing unique payouts for "Four Deuces" and "Five of a Kind."

## Strategy: The "Wild Deuce" Approach

Because all four 2s are wild, your strategy must change from traditional video poker. 
1. **The Single Deuce Hold:** In Loose Deuces, holding a single Deuce is almost always the correct move. This is because the "Expected Value (EV)" of the hands you can complete with a 2 (like Five of a Kind) is so high that it pulls the strategy toward the wild cards.
2. **The "Natural" Royal Flush:** While the 2s are wild, a Royal Flush without a 2 is the "Natural" version, which pays the most. This creates a unique strategic tension: when do you hold for a wild Royal vs. a natural Royal?
3. **High Variance:** Because so much of the RTP is concentrated in the Four Deuces and Royal Flush, you will experience "dry spells" where you don't hit the big hand. An AP ensures they have a massive **Bankroll** to weather the "Standard Deviation" of losses.
