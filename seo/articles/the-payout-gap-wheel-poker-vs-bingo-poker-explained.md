### The Feature Gap: Wheel Spin vs. Bingo Board

In **Wheel Poker**, specific hands trigger a spin of a "Wheel" for bonus payouts or multipliers. In **Bingo Poker**, your poker hand results trigger numbers on a bingo board. This creates a significant **Volatility Normalization** shift. **Strategy Overlap:** There is roughly 60% overlap. In Wheel Poker, you prioritize hands that trigger the wheel above all else. In Bingo Poker, you focus on how the poker hand improves your bingo patterns. This is the essence of **Payout Compression**—the wheel bonus and the bingo board feature dominate their respective EV landscapes.

2.  **Board Awareness:** In Bingo Poker, always be looking for numbers that complete your bingo patterns.
3.  **Return Priority:** Wheel Poker offers a superior theoretical return (99.70%), making it the better choice for the advantage player who wants natural action.
