# The Payout Gap: Jacks or Better vs Deuces Wild Explained

In the world of **best odds video poker**, players often find themselves choosing between two of the most popular machines on the floor: Jacks or Better and Deuces Wild. While they look similar, the **EV Gap** between them depends entirely on their specific pay tables and your tolerance for **House Edge Variance**.

## The Best Odds Index: 9/6 Jacks vs Full Pay Deuces
In the **highest return video poker games** 2026 ranking, both machines offer strong professional returns when found in their "Full Pay" configurations:
*   **9/6 Jacks or Better:** 99.54% RTP.
*   **Full Pay Deuces Wild:** 100.76% RTP.

At first glance, Deuces Wild appears to be the superior choice. However, Deuces Wild offers a "Bonus" for hitting Four Deuces (specifically Four Deuces, which pays 200-for-1 instead of 25-for-1). This change in the pay table structure shifts the **Theoretical Return Convergence**.

## Analysis of "Short Pay" Predation
The real "Payout Gap" occurs when casinos offer sub-optimal versions of these games. 
*   **Jacks or Better (8/5):** RTP falls to 97.30%.
*   **Airport Deuces:** RTP falls to 94.82%.

If you find a 9/6 Jacks machine next to an Airport Deuces machine, the 4.72% **EV Gap** is predatory. A professional player would never touch the Airport Deuces machine in this scenario. However, if the choice is between 8/5 Jacks (97.30%) and Not-So-Ugly Deuces (99.73%), the Deuces Wild machine is actually the much better bet.

## House Edge Variance and Strategy
Deuces Wild has higher variance than Jacks or Better. This is because more of the return is tied up in the Four Deuces and Natural Royal Flush payouts. In Jacks or Better, the Two Pair payout (2-for-1) is the engine that keeps your bankroll alive. 
*   **Jacks or Better Strategy:** Focuses on bankroll preservation and hitting the Royal Flush.
*   **Deuces Wild Strategy:** Slightly adjusts to account for the higher value of Four Deuces, particularly deuces.
