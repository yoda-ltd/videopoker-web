# The Payout Gap: Full Pay Deuces vs. Royal Flush Bonus Explained
Category: Comparison

Understanding the **Strategic Divergence** between **Full Pay Deuces** and **Royal Flush Bonus** is key to mastering these high-payout variants. This comparison focuses on how the paytables differ and why those differences lead to unique **EV Maximization** paths.

### The Wild Gap: Natural vs. Wild Connectivity

In **Full Pay Deuces**, the four 2s are your most powerful tools, allowing you to complete hands that would otherwise be worthless. In **Royal Flush Bonus**, the game is natural, but the payout for a Royal Flush is significantly increased. This creates a higher **Volatility Normalization** (32.0 vs 25.8).

**Strategy Overlap:** There is roughly 40% overlap. In Full Pay Deuces, you prioritize the 2s above all else. In Royal Flush Bonus, you prioritize Royal Flush cards, often breaking winners to maximize the bonus potential. This is the essence of **Payout Compression**—the 2s in Deuces Wild and the Royal in Royal Flush Bonus dominate their respective EV landscapes.

### Strategic Key Shifts
1.  **Deuce Supremacy:** In Full Pay Deuces, never discard a 2. It is your ultimate tool for **EV Maximization**.
2.  **Royal Flush Priority:** In Royal Flush Bonus, prioritize 3-to-a-Royal and 4-to-a-Royal much more than in standard games.
3.  **Return Priority:** Full Pay Deuces offers a superior theoretical return (100.76%), making it the better choice for the advantage player.

The **best video poker strategy comparison** indicates that both games are elite choices for the expert player, but Full Pay Deuces provides a more stable ride. A **video poker house edge analysis** confirms that both offer significant player advantages in their full-pay versions.
