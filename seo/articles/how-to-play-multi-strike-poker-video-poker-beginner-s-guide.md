## How to Play: The Basics

### The 20-Coin Bet
To play all four levels, you must bet 20 coins (5 coins for each of the 4 levels). This wager activates the tiered system and is verified by **RTP Verification** to ensure the increased bet size translates to the correct mathematical return.

### Tiered Multipliers and "Ladder EV"
Multi-Strike is a tiered game. A winning hand on Level 1 (1x multiplier) moves you to Level 2 (2x), then Level 3 (4x), and finally Level 4 (8x). To ensure **game integrity**, the **Random Number Generator (RNG)** must provide an honest deal on every level.

- **The Multiplier Effect**: If you hit a Royal Flush on Level 4, the 8x multiplier turns it into a massive 32,000-coin payout. 
- **RNG Independence**: Every hand on every level is an independent event, guaranteed by a **trusted video poker RNG certification**. The machine does not "make it harder" to win as you move up the ladder.

## Strategy: Survival vs. Aggression

Your strategy in Multi-Strike changes as you climb the levels. This precision is supported by **Jurisdictional Mandates** that require the machine to offer a fair draw at every stage.

### "Survival Play" at Lower Levels
On Level 1 and Level 2, your focus must be on **Survival**. 
- **The Correct Play**: Prioritize high-probability winning hands (like low pairs) to ensure you advance to the next level.
- **The Goal**: Reach the 8x multiplier on Level 4. Chasing a low-probability hand on Level 1 is mathematically incorrect when you consider the "Ladder EV."

### Aggressive Strategy at Level 4
Once you reach Level 4, every payout is multiplied by 8. Since you can't advance any further, your only goal is to maximize the payout of the current hand. Your strategy shifts to the standard optimal play for the base variant (e.g., Jacks or Better).
