# Comparing Super Star Poker to Blackjack and Craps
Category: Comparison

When choosing between **Super Star Poker** video poker, **Blackjack**, and **Craps**, players are essentially deciding between a game of "Multi-Game Multipliers," "Skill-Based Odds," and "Pure Probability." Each game offers a unique path to **EV Maximization**, but the mechanics and house edges differ significantly.

This **best video poker strategy comparison** highlights how the different house edges and skill requirements impact your long-term return.

| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Variance Index** | 45.0+ | Low (~1.2) | Medium (~2.0) |

### Strategic Divergence: Persistent Multipliers vs. Card Totals

In **Super Star Poker**, your results can trigger multipliers that apply to your next several hands. This is a game of **Volatility Normalization** through persistent multipliers across multiple games. In **Blackjack**, your return is skill-dependent, focusing on the total value of your cards versus the dealer's. In **Craps**, your return is primarily determined by the specific bets you place (like Pass Line with Odds), with minimal skill required once the bet is made.

**Strategy Overlap:** There is zero (0%) strategy overlap between these games. While they all aim for **EV Maximization**, the methods are entirely different. In Super Star Poker, you are holding cards to trigger and maximize multipliers; in Blackjack, you are hitting or standing for 21; in Craps, you are placing chips for dice results.

### Key Strategy Adjustments
1.  **Super Star Poker Skill:** You must master the exact strategy for multiplier management. Aggressive play is often required when a high multiplier is active.
2.  **Blackjack Discipline:** You must follow Basic Strategy perfectly to achieve the 0.50% house edge.
3.  **Craps Simplicity:** To achieve the best odds in Craps, you simply place the Pass Line bet and back it with the maximum allowed "Odds" bet. This is a form of **Payout Compression**—the "Odds" bet has zero house edge.

Through a detailed **video poker house edge analysis**, it's clear that Super Star Poker offers a superior return compared to standard Blackjack and Craps, provided you are willing to master the complex strategy and pay the 10th coin feature bet. However, Blackjack offers a much lower variance, making it a smoother ride for the risk-averse player.
