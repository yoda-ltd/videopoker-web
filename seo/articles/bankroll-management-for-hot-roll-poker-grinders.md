Notice that even on a "Quarter" machine, you are betting $2.50 per hand. This is the same as a standard $0.50 machine.

### C. The "Dice" Discipline
The dice rolls are random. Do not "Increase your bet" because you feel like a "Hot Roll" is "Due." The machine has no memory. Stick to your bankroll plan and let the dice rolls happen when they happen.
