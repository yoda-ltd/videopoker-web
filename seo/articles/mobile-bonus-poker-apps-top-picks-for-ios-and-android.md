### 2. Input Latency and Refresh Rate Synchronization
For pro-level grinding, every millisecond counts. **Input Latency**—the delay between your touch and the app's response—can disrupt your rhythm and lead to errors. ### 3. Mobile RNG Parity
Serious players demand **Mobile RNG Parity**. This ensures that the Random Number Generator used in the mobile app is mathematically identical to the land-based machines. Native apps generally offer superior RNG performance and security compared to responsive web wrappers, which can be vulnerable to browser-level lag.
