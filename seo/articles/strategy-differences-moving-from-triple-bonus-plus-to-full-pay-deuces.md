# Strategy Differences: Moving from Triple Bonus Plus to Full Pay Deuces

The move from Triple Bonus Plus to Full Pay Deuces Wild (FPDW) is a move from "Bonus Hunting" to "Wild Card Optimization." Both are "High-Reward" versions of video poker, but they require two completely different mindsets. This **best video poker strategy comparison** examines the **Strategy Bifurcation** and **Variance Scaling** involved in moving between these two "Advantage" formats.

## The Mathematical Foundation: Jackpot vs. Wild

**Triple Bonus Plus** is a "Jackpot" game. It is a Jacks or Better variant that pays massive "Triple" bonuses for Four-of-a-Kind (Quads). To fund these 400-to-1 or 800-to-1 payouts, the game uses heavy **Payout Compression** on common hands like Two-Pair. The **video poker house edge analysis** is dominated by the frequency of hitting these rare Quads.

**Full Pay Deuces Wild** is a "Wild" game. The four Deuces (2s) are the engine of the game. The "Reward" is hitting "Four Deuces" (200-to-1) or a "Natural Royal Flush." The **video poker house edge analysis** is actually "Positive" (100.76%) if played perfectly. The "Risk" is that you get "Nothing" for anything less than Three-of-a-Kind. This is the ultimate form of **Payout Compression**.

## Variance Scaling and the Bankroll Buffer

1.  **Triple Bonus Plus:** The variance is extremely high (often 50+). It is a "Boom or Bust" game. The **Variance Scaling** is steep because so much of the return is tied to rare Quad hits. You need a massive bankroll to survive the "Dry" spells.
2.  **Full Pay Deuces Wild:** The variance is moderate (around 25-30). It is a "Grind" game. Because you win "Wild" hands frequently, your bankroll stays "Fluid." The return is "Smoother" than the "Jackpot or Nothing" nature of Triple Bonus Plus.

## Strategy Bifurcation: The "Hold" Logic

The transition requires a total "Mental Reboot":
*   In **Triple Bonus Plus**, you are a "Specialist." You hold small pairs and high pairs with extreme priority. You accept the "Money Back" nature of Two-Pair as the price of admission.
*   In **Full Pay Deuces Wild**, the "Deuce" is king. You will hold a single Deuce over *everything* else (except a natural Royal draw). You will break apart a "Natural" Straight to keep a lone Deuce.

If you enjoy the "Strike" of a massive jackpot for a specific "Natural" card combination, Triple Bonus Plus is your game. If you prefer the "Mathematics" of a positive-expectation game where your "Hold" decisions determine your success, Full Pay Deuces Wild is the ultimate destination for the serious advantage player.
