## 2. Ignoring "Penalty Card" Awareness in the 53-Card Deck

Joker Poker uses a 53-card deck, which alters the probability of every draw.

- **The Mistake**: Using Jacks or Better "Penalty Card" logic in a Joker Poker game.
- **The Pro Secret**: **Penalty Card Awareness** is different in a 53-card deck. The presence of the Joker in the remaining deck "Dilutes" the probability of hitting natural cards. Pros use specialized Joker Poker strategy software to understand these "Probability Shifts." ## 3. The "Natural Royal" Obsession

Because Joker Poker offers both "Natural" and "Wild" Royal Flushes, players often get "Royal Fever."

- **The Mistake**: Discarding a Joker to "Go for the Natural Royal."
- **The Pro Secret**: Never, ever discard the Joker. This sounds obvious, but players often do it in "Progressive" games where the Natural Royal jackpot is huge. A pro knows that the "Theoretical Value" of the Joker is so high that discarding it is almost never mathematically sound. They remain "EV Purists," even when a massive jackpot is tempting them.
