## The Hunt for Full Pay
The "pay table" is the most critical factor in Ultimate X. A 9/6 (Full House/Flush) game has a much higher return than an 8/5 or 7/5 game. 
*   **Off-Strip Gems:** Casinos like South Point, the Orleans, and Red Rock often feature better pay tables for Ultimate X than the mega-resorts on the Strip.
*   **Downtown Action:** Check the D, Circa, and Four Queens. These properties cater to video poker enthusiasts and often have competitive 3-play and 5-play Ultimate X options.
