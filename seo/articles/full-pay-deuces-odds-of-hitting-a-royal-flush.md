# Full Pay Deuces Odds of Hitting a Royal Flush

Full Pay Deuces Wild is the "Holy Grail" of video poker. With an Expected Value (EV) of **100.76%**, it is one of the few games where the player holds a mathematical edge over the house—provided they use a perfect strategy. Central to this high return is the Royal Flush, but in Deuces Wild, the "Natural" Royal Flush (without deuces) is the ultimate prize.

## The Mathematics of the Natural Royal Flush

In Full Pay Deuces Wild, the payout for a Natural Royal Flush is **800-for-1** (4,000 coins with a 5-coin max bet). While deuces help you hit "Wild Royal Flushes" (usually paying 25-for-1), the Natural Royal is what drives the variance and contributes significantly to the long-term theoretical return.

### Probability and Frequency
The odds of being dealt a Natural Royal Flush on the initial deal are **1 in 649,740**. However, with optimal discard strategy, your actual frequency of hitting one is approximately:

*   **1 in 45,282 hands.**

This is slightly more difficult to hit than in Jacks or Better (approx. 1 in 40,391) because the strategy in Deuces Wild often prioritizes holding deuces over holding small Royal components.

## Pay Table Impact on EV
A "Full Pay" table is defined by the following key payouts:
*   **Natural Royal Flush:** 800:1
*   **Four Deuces:** 200:1
*   **Wild Royal Flush:** 25:1
*   **Five of a Kind:** 15:1
*   **Straight Flush:** 9:1
*   **Four of a Kind:** 5:1
*   **Full House:** 3:1

If you see a version where the Four of a Kind pays 4:1 instead of 5:1, the EV drops significantly below 100%, and your strategy for chasing the Royal Flush must shift to account for the increased house edge.

## Strategy Chart: Chasing the Royal with Deuces
When you are dealt a hand with Royal Flush potential, the presence of Deuces changes everything.

| Hand Dealt | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Natural Royal Flush** | Hold All (Obviously) |
| **4 to a Natural Royal** | Hold 4, Discard 1 |
| **3 to a Natural Royal** | Hold 3, unless you have a Deuce |
| **Deuce + 3 to Natural Royal** | Hold Deuce and Royal pieces (unless you have a pair) |

### Why "Deuces First" Matters
In Full Pay Deuces Wild, a single Deuce is more valuable than almost any unmade hand. If you have a choice between holding a 3-card Royal Flush and a single Deuce, the **Expected Value** of the Deuce is higher. Never discard a Deuce to chase a Natural Royal Flush.
