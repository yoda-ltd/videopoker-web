## Understanding Tribal Compacts

A **Tribal Compact** is an agreement between a federally recognized tribe and a state government that dictates the types of games allowed and the revenue-sharing arrangements.

- **Class III Gaming**: These are "Las Vegas style" games, including traditional video poker where the RNG (Random Number Generator) mimics a fair 52-card deck.
- **Class II Gaming**: Often found in tribal hubs where state compacts are more restrictive, these machines look like video poker but are actually based on a central bingo server. 

**Critical Strategy Tip**: In Class II "Bingo-based" video poker, your strategy doesn't actually matter for the outcome—the "win" is determined by the bingo drawing. Always check if the machine is Class II before applying a complex strategy.
