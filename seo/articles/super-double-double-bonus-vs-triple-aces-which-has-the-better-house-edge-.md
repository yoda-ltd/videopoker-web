To choose the right game, you must understand how **video poker fairness and regulation** ensures the integrity of these specialized high-volatility models.

## The Mathematical Framework: Kickers vs. Rank-Focus

The house edge in these games is determined by the "Path to the Bonus."

*   **Super Double Double Bonus:** Value is spread across multiple ranks (Aces through 4s). You receive massive payouts (e.g., 2,000 coins for 4 Aces) if you hold a valid kicker. The edge is tied to your mastery of the complex "Kicker-Targeting" strategy.
