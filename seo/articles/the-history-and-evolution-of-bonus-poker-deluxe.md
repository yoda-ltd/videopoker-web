# The History and Evolution of Bonus Poker Deluxe

Bonus Poker Deluxe is the "refined" version of the original Bonus Poker. It represents a significant moment in video poker history where developers realized that players wanted a "simpler" bonus experience—one where the math was easier to follow and the rewards were more predictable.

## The "Simplification" Movement

In the early 1990s, the video poker market was flooded with "Double Bonus" and "Triple Bonus" games. These games were exciting, but the pay tables were complex, with different payouts for different types of Four of a Kind.

Bonus Poker Deluxe was developed as the "Pure" alternative. Its primary evolution was making **all Four of a Kind hands pay the same amount** (usually 400 credits at a 5-coin bet). This was a significant departure from standard Bonus Poker, where Aces paid more than 2s, 3s, and 4s.

## Key Evolutionary Milestones

1.  **The "80-for-1" Standard:** Bonus Poker Deluxe helped establish the "80-for-1" payout for quads as a "Gold Standard" for stability. By making every quad worth the same, the "stress" of hitting "the wrong kind of four-of-a-kind" was eliminated.
2.  **The "Two Pair" Sacrifice:** To fund the higher payout for quads (standard Jacks or Better pays 125 credits; Bonus Deluxe pays 400), developers had to make a sacrifice. In Bonus Poker Deluxe, the "Two Pair" payout is reduced to 1-for-1 (even money).
3.  **Cross-Platform Success:** Because of its simplicity, Bonus Poker Deluxe became one of the first games to be successfully ported to early mobile and hand-held video poker devices.

## Video Poker Terms Every Player Should Know

When searching the complete video poker glossary, these terms are vital for Bonus Poker Deluxe:

*   **Bankroll Management:** Because Two Pair only pays even money, your bankroll will drop faster than in Jacks or Better. You need a "Resilient Bankroll" to survive until the next 400-credit quad.
*   **Variance:** Bonus Poker Deluxe is considered "Medium-High" variance. It's the perfect middle ground between the "Safe" play of Jacks or Better and the "Wild" play of Double Double Bonus.
*   **Full Pay:** The classic Full Pay version (9/6 table) offers a return of 99.64%.

## Strategy and "Quad Hunting"

The evolution of Bonus Poker Deluxe created a "Quad-Hunting" strategy. Since all quads are worth the same, players don't have to worry about "Ace-Heavy" or "Face-Card" strategies. The goal is simple: **Get any four of a kind.** This made the game incredibly popular with casual players who found the strategy for "Triple Double Bonus" too intimidating.

## Modern Status

Today, Bonus Poker Deluxe is a permanent fixture on the IGT "Game King" platform. It is often the "Default" bonus game for many players because it offers a "High Reward" feel without the "High Confusion" of complex pay tables.

The history of Bonus Poker Deluxe is a lesson in the power of "Balanced Design." It remains one of the most respected and played games in the "Complete Video Poker Glossary."
