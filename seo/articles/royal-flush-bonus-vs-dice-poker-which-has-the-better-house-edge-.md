# Royal Flush Bonus vs. Dice Poker: Which Has the Better House Edge?

Comparing **Royal Flush Bonus** and **Dice Poker** is a comparison of two extremes: the ultimate card-based jackpot vs. a simplified visual dice model. While Royal Flush Bonus focuses on a rare, high-value hit, Dice Poker uses a smaller mathematical pool to provide frequent wins. To choose the right game, you must understand how **video poker fairness and regulation** ensures the integrity of these contrasting models.

## The Mathematical Framework: Jackpots vs. Distributions

The house edge in these games is determined by the "Payout Curve."

*   **Royal Flush Bonus:** Value is heavily weighted toward a single 1-in-40,000 hand. The edge is tied to the player's patience and ability to survive long periods of "bankroll decay" while waiting for the bonus hit.
*   **Dice Poker:** Value is distributed more evenly across hands like Three-of-a-Kind and Straights. With only 7,776 initial outcomes, the mathematical distribution is tighter and more predictable.
