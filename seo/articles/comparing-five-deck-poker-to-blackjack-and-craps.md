# Comparing Five Deck Poker to Blackjack and Craps

Five Deck Poker is a unique video poker variant that uses five separate decks for each hand. This creates a different set of probabilities than standard single-deck games. But how does it stack up against Blackjack and Craps?

### RTP Comparison
- **Five Deck Poker**: The **Theoretical Return** depends heavily on the specific pay table, but it typically ranges from 98% to 99%.
- **Blackjack**: Offers a 99.5% RTP (0.5% house edge) with perfect basic strategy.
- **Craps (Pass Line)**: Offers a 98.59% RTP (1.41% house edge).

While Blackjack still offers the best base odds, Five Deck Poker provides a more varied and statistically unique experience that appeals to many players.

### Variance and the Five Decks
Using five decks increases the frequency of certain hands while making others rarer. This changes the **Variance** profile of the game. For **Session Sustainability**, you need to understand how the five-deck structure affects your bankroll. Generally, you need more **Variance Buffering** than you would for a standard game of Craps to handle the unique swings of the multiple decks.

### "Is Max Bet Always Better for Bankroll Longevity?"
In Five Deck Poker, as in almost all video poker, the 5th coin is essential for the top-tier payouts. For those looking for **video poker tips for beginners**, our advice is always the same: play max bet or find a different game. Without the 5th coin, the RTP of Five Deck Poker falls well below that of Blackjack or Craps.

### Strategy and Hit Frequency
The strategy for Five Deck Poker is significantly different from single-deck games. **Hit Frequency Optimization** must account for the fact that there are five of every card in the total pool. This requires a specialized strategy chart. It is more complex to learn than Craps but offers a more solitary and focused gaming experience.

### RNG Reality
Some players believe that with five decks, the **Pseudo-Randomness** is easier to "predict" or "track." This is a myth. The RNG still treats every card selection as an independent event. The five-deck structure is simply a different mathematical model, not a change in the fairness or randomness of the game.

If you want to know **how to win video poker daily**, Five Deck Poker offers a refreshing and mathematically interesting alternative to the standard table games. Just make sure you are using the right strategy for the right number of decks.
