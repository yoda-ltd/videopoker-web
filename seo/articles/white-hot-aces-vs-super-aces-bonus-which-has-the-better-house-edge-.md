# White Hot Aces vs. Super Aces Bonus: Which Has the Better House Edge?

Comparing **White Hot Aces** and **Super Aces Bonus** is a study in rank concentration. While both games focus on hitting high-ranking Four-of-a-Kind hands involving Aces, they differ in how they value those combinations. White Hot Aces offers a high but balanced return for Aces, while Super Aces Bonus provides an even higher, "Super" jackpot for Four Aces. To choose the right game, you must understand how **video poker fairness and regulation** ensures the integrity of these specialized "Ace-Centric" models.

## The Mathematical Framework: High Ranks vs. Super Jackpots

The house edge in these games is determined by the "Concentration of Value."

*   **White Hot Aces:** Value is concentrated in hitting Four Aces (usually 240-for-1). The edge comes from the player's ability to prioritize Aces while maintaining a high return on standard hands.
*   **Super Aces Bonus:** Value is even more concentrated at the top. Hitting Four Aces pays 400-for-1 (2,000 coins at max bet). The edge comes from the player's ability to navigate the increased volatility of a "Jackpot-Grinder" game.
