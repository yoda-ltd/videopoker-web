# Triple Bonus Plus vs. Triple Aces: Which Has the Better House Edge?

Comparing **Triple Bonus Plus** and **Triple Aces** is a study in reward distribution. While Triple Bonus Plus rewards you for hitting high-ranking Four-of-a-Kind combinations across multiple ranks, Triple Aces concentrates its massive "Bonus" potential into hitting Four Aces. To choose the right game, you must understand how **video poker fairness and regulation** ensures the integrity of these specialized "High-Quad" mathematical models.

## The Mathematical Framework: Distributed vs. Concentrated Value

The house edge in these games is determined by how the "Big Wins" are tiered.

*   **Triple Bonus Plus:** Value is distributed more evenly across the top of the pay table. You receive high payouts for Aces (e.g., 120-for-1) and strong bonuses for 2s, 3s, and 4s. This creates more frequent "Jackpot" events.
*   **Triple Aces:** Value is heavily concentrated in the Four Aces payout, which often pays as much as a Royal Flush (800-for-1). The edge comes from the player's ability to prioritize Aces above all other ranks in their strategy.
