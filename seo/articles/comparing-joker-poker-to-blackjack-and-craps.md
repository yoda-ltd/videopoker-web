Each game offers a unique path to **EV Maximization**, but the mechanics and house edges differ significantly.

| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

### Strategic Divergence: Wild Connectivity vs. Card Totals

In **Joker Poker**, the single Joker is your most powerful tool, allowing you to complete hands that would otherwise be worthless. This is a game of **Volatility Normalization** through wild card connectivity. In **Blackjack**, your return is skill-dependent, focusing on the total value of your cards versus the dealer's. **Strategy Overlap:** There is zero (0%) strategy overlap between these games. While they all aim for **EV Maximization**, the methods are entirely different. In Joker Poker, you are holding cards for wild card connectivity; in Blackjack, you are hitting or standing for 21; in Craps, you are placing chips for dice results.

### Key Strategy Adjustments
1.  **Joker Poker Skill:** You must master the exact strategy for wild card connectivity. The Joker is never discarded.
2.  **Blackjack Discipline:** You must follow Basic Strategy perfectly to achieve the 0.50% house edge.
3.  **Craps Simplicity:** To achieve the best odds in Craps, you simply place the Pass Line bet and back it with the maximum allowed "Odds" bet. This is a form of **Payout Compression**—the "Odds" bet has zero house edge.

Through a detailed **video poker house edge analysis**, it's clear that Joker Poker offers a superior return compared to standard Blackjack and Craps, provided you are willing to master the complex strategy. However, Blackjack offers a much lower variance, making it a smoother ride for the risk-averse player.
