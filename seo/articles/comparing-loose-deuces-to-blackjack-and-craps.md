# Comparing Loose Deuces to Blackjack and Craps

Loose Deuces is a popular video poker variant that offers high payouts for Four-of-a-Kind Deuces. How does this high-action game compare to the "big two" of the casino: Blackjack and Craps?

### Theoretical Return Battle
- **Loose Deuces**: A full-pay machine can offer a **Theoretical Return** of over 100% (specifically 100.15% on some configurations).
- **Blackjack**: Offers a 99.5% RTP (0.5% house edge) with perfect basic strategy.
- **Craps (Pass Line)**: Offers a 98.59% RTP (1.41% house edge).

Loose Deuces is one of the rare games where, with perfect strategy, you can actually have an advantage over the house. This makes it a significantly better mathematical bet than both Blackjack and Craps.

### Variance and Sustainability
The trade-off for the high RTP is extreme **Variance**. Loose Deuces is much more volatile than Blackjack or Craps because a massive portion of its return is tied to hitting Four Deuces or a Royal Flush. For **Session Sustainability**, you need a much larger bankroll for **Variance Buffering**. A "dry spell" in Loose Deuces will be much deeper than one in Blackjack.

### The Max Bet Essential
"Is max bet always better for bankroll longevity?" In Blackjack and Craps, your bet size doesn't change the house edge. In Loose Deuces, the 5th coin is mandatory to get the full Royal Flush bonus and the >100% RTP. If you aren't playing max bet, the RTP drops well below that of a standard game of Craps.

### Strategy and Hit Frequency
Loose Deuces requires a very specific strategy that focuses on **Hit Frequency Optimization** for the deuces. This is more complex than Blackjack's basic strategy. For those looking for **video poker tips for beginners**, our advice is to master standard Deuces Wild before tackling the higher volatility of Loose Deuces.

### RNG vs. Table Randomness
While Blackjack and Craps involve physical elements, Loose Deuces relies on **Pseudo-Randomness** through a certified RNG. This ensures the game is fair and every hand is independent. To learn **how to win video poker daily**, you must trust the RNG and focus entirely on your mathematical strategy.

Loose Deuces is a hidden gem for the serious gambler. If you can find a full-pay machine and have the bankroll for the swings, it is one of the few games where the advantage is truly yours.
