# The History and Evolution of Triple Double Bonus Poker: The High-Volatility Legend

In the world of video poker, *Double Double Bonus Poker* is already known as a high-volatility variant. But for some players, it wasn't enough. They wanted more action, more risk, and more reward. In the mid-1990s, the "Triple Double Bonus" variant appeared to fill that demand. The **history of Triple Double Bonus video poker** is a journey into the "high-stakes" world that redefined the player experience. In this article, we explore the **evolution of video poker games** through the lens of a "triple" powerhouse.

## The Origin: The Mid-1990s Payout Shift
The **history of Triple Double Bonus video poker** begins in **1997-1998**. At the time, IGT’s *Double Double Bonus Poker* was a massive hit, but players were becoming accustomed to the "Double" formula. **International Game Technology (IGT)** and other manufacturers like **Action Gaming** (the pioneers of Multi-Play) introduced **Triple Double Bonus** as a high-volatility variant of *Double Double Bonus*.

The **Digital Logic Transition** was significant: players could win as much for "Four Aces with a Kicker" as they could for a Royal Flush (800-for-1). This required a completely different mathematical approach, as the game's house edge became very top-heavy.

## The Manufacturer: IGT and the Action Gaming Connection
**International Game Technology (IGT)** and **Action Gaming** (led by Ernie Moody) were the primary drivers of the Triple Double Bonus theme. The machine featured graphics that emphasized the "Triple" nature of the game and often used sound effects like a bell or a fanfare for every Four Aces with a Kicker hit. 

The **Casino Adoption Curve** for Triple Double Bonus was unlike any other. It quickly became the "locals' favorite" in Nevada. For a player who sat at a machine for hours, the 800-for-1 payout for "Aces with a Kicker" was the ultimate goal. Casinos loved it because it increased "coin-in"—players were willing to play faster and longer to chase the kicker.

## Why Triple Double Bonus Became "Legendary"
While many variants remained niche experiments, Triple Double Bonus became a legend for its:
1.  **High Volatility:** Triple Double Bonus is one of the most volatile games in the casino. While players can win big, they can also go long periods without a payout. This made the game a favorite for "educated" gamblers who were willing to risk their bankrolls for the chance at a massive win.
2.  **Strategy Complexity:** Triple Double Bonus strategy is significantly different from Jacks or Better. Because Four Aces with a Kicker pays 800-for-1, players must learn when to hold a kicker with *any* three Aces—a move that is mathematically incorrect in almost any other game. This skill gap made Triple Double Bonus a favorite for "educated" gamblers.
3.  **Visual and Sound Feedback:** IGT’s machines were programmed with unique sound effects for the 800-for-1 win, making every "Four Aces with a Kicker" hit a floor-wide event.

## Pay Table Evolution: The "Full Pay" 9/7 Standard
The **history of Triple Double Bonus video poker** is defined by its "Full Pay" version (often called 9/7):
*   **Full House:** 9 coins
*   **Flush:** 7 coins
*   **Four Aces with Kicker (2, 3, 4):** 800 coins
*   **Four Aces (No Kicker):** 160 coins
*   **Four 2s, 3s, 4s with Kicker (A-4):** 160 coins

With perfect strategy, the 9/7 version offers an RTP of **99.58%**. This was a solid return, though higher than the 9/6 Jacks or Better (99.54%).

## SEO and the Evolution of Video Poker
When discussing the **evolution of video poker games**, Triple Double Bonus is often cited as the first "jackpot-heavy" variant. It proved that a simple kicker mechanic and a specialized payout table could create a loyal following. This **Patent History** of kicker-based payout modification would lead to modern games like *Super Triple Play* and *Wheel Poker*.

## Conclusion
The **history of Triple Double Bonus video poker** is a story of a game that tripled the "Double Bonus" formula and won big. By rewarding players with 800-for-1 for Four Aces with a Kicker, IGT and Action Gaming created a variant that remains a favorite for both casual and professional players. Today, Triple Double Bonus continues to be a staple on **Multi-Game** machines and in many online casinos, continuing its legacy as the ultimate "High-Volatility" video poker experience.
