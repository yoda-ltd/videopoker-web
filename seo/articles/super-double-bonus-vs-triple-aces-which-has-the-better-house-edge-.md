# Super Double Bonus vs. Triple Aces: Which Has the Better House Edge?

Comparing **Super Double Bonus** and **Triple Aces** is a study in rank distribution. While Super Double Bonus rewards you for hitting high-ranking Four-of-a-Kind hands across multiple ranks (Aces through 4s), Triple Aces concentrates its value heavily in the Four Aces payout. To choose the right game, you must understand how **video poker fairness and regulation** ensures the integrity of these specialized "High-Quad" mathematical models.

## The Mathematical Framework: Distribution vs. Focus

The house edge in these games is determined by how the "Big Wins" are spread across the ranks.

*   **Super Double Bonus:** Value is distributed more evenly. Aces pay 160-for-1, while 2s, 3s, and 4s pay 80-for-1. This provides more frequent "medium-sized" jackpot hits.
*   **Triple Aces:** Value is heavily focused on the Four Aces payout, which often rivals or matches the Royal Flush. The edge comes from the player's ability to prioritize Aces above all other ranks in their strategic holds.
