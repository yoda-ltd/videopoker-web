## Defining Market Saturation in Mississippi

**Market Saturation** occurs when the supply of casino gaming (machines and tables) exceeds the demand of the local and regional population. 
*   **Tunica:** Currently experiencing "High Saturation/Low Demand." Tunica has seen property closures in the last decade, leaving a smaller, leaner market that must fight desperately for every player.
*   **Biloxi:** Experiencing "Healthy Saturation/High Demand." Biloxi benefits from coastal tourism and a growing retirement population, allowing for a more stable and luxurious gaming environment.

## Impact on Video Poker Pay Tables

In Biloxi, the healthy demand allows casinos to be slightly more "conservative" with their base pay tables, but they compensate with better overall amenities.
*   **The Result:** While you can still find **Full-Pay Inventory** in Biloxi (especially at the IP and Beau Rivage), the machines are often spread out, and you may find 8/5 Bonus Poker where Tunica would offer 8/6.
