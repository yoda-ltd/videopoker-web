# Psychology of Playing Bonus Poker: Staying Focused

Bonus Poker is a more volatile version of Jacks or Better, with increased payouts for four-of-a-kind. This change in the pay table creates a unique psychological environment that demands a **Pro Mindset** and intense **Professional Discipline**.

## The High-Reward Trap: The Dopamine Loop of Bonus Payouts

In Bonus Poker, four-of-a-kind (especially for Aces through Fours) is a major goal. This leads to a powerful **Dopamine Loop**. 

- **Dopamine Loop**: The "Bonus" in Bonus Poker is designed to trigger a stronger neurochemical response when you hit the big hand.
- **Expert Video Poker Strategy Tips**: To succeed, you must avoid the "Chasing" mentality. Do not hold two Aces in Bonus Poker because you "feel" like four Aces are coming. Stick to the mathematically correct play every time.
- **Loss Aversion Bias**: Players who lose back-to-back sessions in Bonus Poker are more likely to "Tilt" because they feel they "should" have hit a Four of a Kind by now. 

## Handling the "Royal Desert" in Bonus Poker

The Royal Flush is still the largest payout in Bonus Poker, but the game is less reliant on it than other variants. However, going on a **Royal Desert** can still be a major psychological blow.

1. **Cognitive Resilience**: You must be able to withstand 100,000 hands without a Royal Flush, provided you are hitting enough Four of a Kinds to keep your bankroll healthy.
2. **The Assembly Line**: Treat each hand as a part of a million-hand sequence. This perspective is vital for **Tilt Control**.
3. **Professional Discipline**: Do not let a "Royal Desert" affect your strategy. If the math says to throw away a "Near-Miss" for a Royal to hold a pair of Aces, you must do it.

## Avoiding Session Fatigue and Managing Your Edge

**Session Fatigue** is particularly dangerous in Bonus Poker because the strategy differs slightly from Jacks or Better. 

- **How to Become a Professional Video Poker Player**: A pro knows the subtle differences between these games and can switch between them without error.
- **Responsible Video Poker Playing**: If you are playing Bonus Poker because "the machine feels hot," you have lost your professional edge. **Tilt Mitigation** means walking away when your emotions start to dictate your play.

## Professional Discipline and the Pro Mindset

- **The Mindset Shift**: Stop viewing the game as "gambling" and start viewing it as "extracting value from a mathematical edge." 
- **Control the Tilt**: If a missed Four of a Kind makes you want to increase your bet to "catch up," you are on "Tilt."

## Conclusion
Bonus Poker is a game of highs and lows. By focusing on **Tilt Control** and maintaining a rigorous **Pro Mindset**, you can harness the "Bonus" in this game and maintain your professional edge.
