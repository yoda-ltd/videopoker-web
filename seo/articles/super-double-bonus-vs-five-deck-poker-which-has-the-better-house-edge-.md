# Super Double Bonus vs. Five Deck Poker: Which Has the Better House Edge?

Comparing **Super Double Bonus** and **Five Deck Poker** is a study in rank-specific bonuses vs. structural deck expansion. While Super Double Bonus rewards you for hitting high-value Four-of-a-Kind combinations, Five Deck Poker uses multiple decks to enable hands that are impossible in standard variants. To choose the right game, you must understand how **video poker fairness and regulation** ensures the integrity of these specialized mathematical models.

## The Mathematical Framework: Quads vs. Parallel Decks

The house edge in these games is determined by the "Payout Distribution."

*   **Super Double Bonus:** Value is concentrated in the 4-of-a-kind hands (Aces pay 160-for-1, 2s-4s pay 80-for-1). The edge is tied to the player's ability to navigate the complex "Rank-Targeting" strategy.
*   **Five Deck Poker:** Value is distributed across hands like "Five-of-a-Kind." The edge is tied to the player's ability to master the unique probabilities of duplicate cards appearing across five parallel deck shuffles.
