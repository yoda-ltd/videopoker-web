# ADT Scoping and Mailer Equity: The Science of High-Volume Video Poker

In the world of advantage gambling, the game on the screen is only half the battle. The real profit is often found in the **Mailer Equity** generated by your **ADT (Average Daily Theoretical)**. Understanding how to "scope" your play to trigger specific casino offers is the difference between a break-even grinder and a professional comp hustler.

## 1. What is ADT? (Average Daily Theoretical)

**ADT** is the single most important number in a casino's database. It represents the "Theoretical Loss" the casino expects to win from you over a 24-hour period.

*   **Formula:** Coin-In × House Edge = ADT.
*   **The Nuance:** If you play $10,000 on a machine that the casino "rates" as a 2% house edge, your ADT is $200. This is true even if you actually *win* $1,000 that day. The casino rewards you based on the math, not your luck.

## 2. ADT Scoping: Triggering the Right Offers

Casinos have different "buckets" for their rewards. To maximize your **best video poker rewards strategy**, you must scope your play to hit the threshold of the offer you want.

*   **Bucket 1 ($50-$150 ADT):** Triggers basic "Bounce Back" free play and mid-week room offers.
*   **Bucket 2 ($200-$500 ADT):** Triggers "Full RFB" (Room, Food, Beverage) and weekend suites.
*   **Bucket 3 ($1,000+ ADT):** Triggers "Host-Level" comps, airfare reimbursements, and VIP event invites.

**The Strategy:** Do not "overplay." If a $300 ADT gets you the suite you want, playing to a $450 ADT is "wasted equity." Save that bankroll for your next trip to keep your numbers consistent.

## 3. Understanding Mailer Equity

**Mailer Equity** is the projected value of the offers you will receive in the next 30-90 days based on your current play. 

*   **Theoretical Loss Optimization:** If you play a 99.5% machine (0.5% edge), but the casino rates it at 2%, you are "earning" 1.5% in **Mailer Equity** that the casino doesn't realize it's giving away. 
*   **The Math:** $10,000 coin-in generates $200 in "Theo" for the casino. If they give back 30% in mailers, you get $60 in Free Play/Food. Your *actual* loss on that $10k was only $50. You are now playing a +EV game.

## 4. The Danger of "Zero-Play Days"

A "Zero-Play Day" is a day you are on a casino property but do not record any play. 

*   **The Trap:** If you stay 3 nights and play $3,000 on Day 1, $3,000 on Day 2, and $0 on Day 3, your ADT is $2,000.
*   **The Solution:** If you had played $3,000 on Day 1, $3,000 on Day 2, and *did not stay at the hotel* on Day 3, your ADT would be $3,000. 
*   **Professional Tip:** If you must stay a third night without playing, do not use your player's card for *any* transaction. Pay cash for dinner and don't visit the kiosk.

## 5. Tier Matching and Mailer Continuity

If you are looking for **how to get free rooms in Las Vegas video poker**, use **Tier Matching** to bridge the gap between operators.
