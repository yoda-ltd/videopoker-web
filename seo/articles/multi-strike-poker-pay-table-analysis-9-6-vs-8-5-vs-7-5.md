# Multi-Strike Poker Pay Table Analysis: 9/6 vs. 8/5 vs. 7/5
Category: Pay Table Analysis

Multi-Strike Poker, an IGT-patented classic, is a high-octane variant that combines the skill-based strategy of video poker with tiered multipliers. For those who want to maximize their returns, understanding the paytable is critical. A standard 9/6 (Full House/Flush) paytable for Jacks or Better-style Multi-Strike is the gold standard, offering an overall RTP that can exceed 99.7% with perfect play.

## Paytable Analysis: The Difference Between 9/6, 8/5, and 7/5

### 9/6 (Full House/Flush): The Gold Standard
A 9/6 paytable is the most favorable for Multi-Strike players. With perfect play, the house edge is as low as 0.3%. 
- **The Similarities**: Like all 9/6 paytables, this offers a standard 1,000-coin payout for a Royal Flush at Level 1 (1x multiplier). 
- **The Differences**: In Multi-Strike, the 9/6 paytable is even more valuable because the 8x multiplier on Level 4 (8,000 coins) significantly offsets the 20-coin bet.

### 8/5 (Full House/Flush): The Middle Ground
An 8/5 paytable is less favorable than a 9/6. With perfect play, the RTP drops to approximately 98.4%. 
- **The Similarities**: Both 9/6 and 8/5 paytables offer the same tiered multipliers. 
- **The Differences**: The **Ladder EV** drops significantly when the base hand payouts are reduced, making it much harder to sustain a bankroll through the levels.

### 7/5 (Full House/Flush): The Low-End
A 7/5 paytable is the least favorable for Multi-Strike players. With perfect play, the RTP drops to approximately 97.2%. 
- **The Similarities**: All paytables have the same "Free Ride" and "Bonus Streak" mechanics. 
- **The Differences**: The 7/5 paytable is significantly less efficient than a 9/6. The **Hand-Over-Hand ROI** is much lower, and the **Bonus Trigger Frequency** is reduced.

## The "Free Ride" and "Bonus Streak" Edge

A key differentiator for Multi-Strike is the "Free Ride" and "Bonus Streak" mechanics. These features ensure you advance to the next level regardless of the hand's outcome, drastically improving your **Hand-Over-Hand ROI**.
- **Free Ride Impact**: In Multi-Strike, a Free Ride is a "guaranteed win" for advancement purposes. 
- **Bonus Streak Tips**: Many Multi-Strike machines feature "Bonus Streak" payouts for consecutive wins. Using **bonus streak video poker tips** to maintain these multipliers can lead to a much higher **Bonus Trigger Frequency**.

## Strategy for Different Paytables

### "Survival Play" at Lower Levels
On Level 1 and Level 2, your focus must be on **Survival**, regardless of the paytable. 
- **The Correct Play**: Prioritize high-probability winning hands at the bottom of the ladder to reach the 8x multiplier on Level 4.
- **The Pitfall**: Chasing a low-probability, high-payout hand on Level 1.

### Aggressive Strategy at Level 4
Once you reach Level 4, every payout is multiplied by 8. 
- **Strategy Shift**: At Level 4, your **multi strike video poker strategy** becomes aggressive. Since you can't advance any further, your only goal is to maximize the payout of the current hand.

## Conclusion

Understanding the paytable is the single biggest factor in your overall RTP for Multi-Strike Poker. By seeking out 9/6 (or better) paytables and mastering the **multi strike video poker strategy**, you can turn this 20-coin variant into a professional-grade advantage. Remember: focus on survival early, and go for the kill when the 8x multiplier is active!
