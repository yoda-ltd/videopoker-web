# Comparing Ultimate X to Blackjack and Craps

Ultimate X is a high-stakes, high-action video poker variant that offers multipliers for winning hands. But how does this multiplier-heavy game compare to the classic table games?

### RTP and the Multipliers
- **Ultimate X**: The **Theoretical Return** can be very high, often exceeding 99% on well-configured machines, provided you understand how to "vulture" the multipliers.
- **Blackjack**: Offers a 99.5% RTP (0.5% house edge) with perfect basic strategy.
- **Craps (Pass Line)**: Offers a 98.59% RTP (1.41% house edge).

While Blackjack still holds a slight edge on base RTP, Ultimate X provides the chance for a massive multiplier on any hand, which can lead to incredible payouts.

### Variance and Sustainability
Ultimate X has extreme **Variance** because you have to place an extra bet (usually 10 coins instead of 5) to activate the multiplier feature. To achieve **Session Sustainability**, you need a much larger bankroll for **Variance Buffering** than you would for a standard game of Blackjack. Without the extra bankroll, the cost of the 10th coin will drain your funds.

### "Is Max Bet Always Better for Bankroll Longevity?"
In Ultimate X, the answer is an absolute yes. You must bet the 10 coins to get the multiplier feature. For those looking for **video poker tips for beginners**, our advice is to only play Ultimate X if you can afford the 10-coin bet. Without it, you're better off playing Craps.

### Skill and Strategy
Unlike Blackjack, where you must make the right decision on every hand, Ultimate X requires a unique type of strategy that involves understanding how the multipliers are generated and applied. This is more complex than Blackjack and significantly more challenging than the decisions in Craps.

### RNG vs. Physical Odds
While Craps relies on physical dice and Blackjack on a physical or shuffled deck, Ultimate X uses **Pseudo-Randomness** through an RNG. This ensures that every hand is fair and independent, just like the roll of the dice in a certified game of Craps.

If you want to know **how to win video poker daily**, understanding these comparisons is vital. Ultimate X offers a great balance of risk and reward for those who enjoy high-velocity action and multipliers.
