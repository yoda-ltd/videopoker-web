# The History and Evolution of Double Double Bonus

Double Double Bonus (DDB) is arguably the most popular video poker game in the world today. It represents the "Perfect Storm" of game design—balancing the thrill of a big jackpot with the strategy of a skill-based game.

## The "Kicker" Revolution

Introduced in the early 1990s, Double Double Bonus was the successor to Double Bonus. While Double Bonus made Aces more valuable, DDB introduced a radical new mechanic: **The Kicker**.

If you hit Four of a Kind with a specific fifth card (the "Kicker"), your payout would be doubled. For example, Four Aces with a 2, 3, or 4 kicker paid 2,000 credits—half the value of a Royal Flush.

## Why It Conquered the Casino Floor

1.  **The "Royal Flush" Frequency:** In a standard game, you only feel the "Big Thrill" once every 40,000 hands (the Royal Flush cycle). In DDB, because Four-Aces-with-a-Kicker occurs more frequently, players feel like they are "Always in the Hunt" for a massive win.
2.  **Psychological Engagement:** The "Kicker" added a second layer of excitement to the "Draw." If you were dealt three Aces, you weren't just praying for the fourth Ace; you were also praying for a "Low Card" kicker to maximize the win.
3.  **The "10/6" Standard:** The Full Pay version of DDB (10/6 table) offers a return of 100.07%, making it a favorite for "Locals" and professional "Grinders" who want an edge.

## Essential Video Poker Terminology

When consulting the complete video poker glossary, these terms are vital for Double Double Bonus:

*   **Kicker:** The card that determines the payout level of your Four of a Kind. In DDB, the 2, 3, and 4 are the "Golden Kickers."
*   **Variance:** DDB has "Very High" variance. This is not a game for a small bankroll. You can lose hundreds of dollars in a single "dry spell."
*   **Action:** DDB is the ultimate "Action" game. It creates the loud, celebratory environment that casinos love and players crave.

## Strategy and "Kicker Logic"

The evolution of DDB created a "Kicker-Focused" strategy. Players learned to never discard a potential kicker if they had a pair of Aces, 2s, 3s, or 4s. This added a "Mini-Game" to every hand, increasing the "Time on Device" and the overall enjoyment of the game.

## Modern Status

Today, Double Double Bonus is the "Standard" for almost every video poker tournament and high-stakes floor. It has spawned dozens of sequels, including "Triple Double Bonus" and "Ultimate X Double Double Bonus."

The history of Double Double Bonus is a reminder that in gaming, "More is Better." By adding one simple card (the Kicker), developers created a game that has defined the casino experience for over three decades. It is the "Crown Jewel" of the "Complete Video Poker Glossary."
