# Bonus Poker Deluxe Tournaments: Rules and Winning Tactics

Video Poker tournaments are an exciting way to play the game with a competitive twist. In a **Bonus Poker Deluxe tournament**, players compete against each other for a prize pool based on their performance over a set number of hands or time. 

While the basic rules of the game remain the same, the strategy for a tournament is drastically different than the strategy for regular "live" play. In a tournament, the Goal is to **maximize your total score** within the time limit, not to play for a long-term mathematical return.

## How Bonus Poker Deluxe Tournaments Work

In a typical tournament, all players start with the same number of credits (e.g., 1,000) and a set amount of time (e.g., 15 minutes). The player with the highest total credits at the end of the time wins.

### Why Bonus Poker Deluxe?
Casinos love Bonus Poker Deluxe for tournaments because of the **80-for-1 Quad payout**. This 400-coin win (on a 5-coin bet) happens frequently enough to keep the leaderboard dynamic. A single Four of a Kind can catapult a player from the bottom to the top in an instant.

## Tournament Strategy vs. Live Strategy

The most important thing to understand is that in a tournament, **you are not playing with real money**. The credits are just points. This means the normal rules of "protecting your bankroll" or "playing for the best EV" do not apply.

### 1. Speed is Key
In most tournaments, you have more credits than you can possibly spend in the allotted time. The goal is to play **as many hands as possible**. 
- Don't stop to admire a win.
- Don't overthink your strategy.
- Speed through the draw and deal.
Every hand you *don't* play is a lost opportunity for a Royal Flush or a Quad.

### 2. High Variance is Your Friend
In a tournament, finishing in 10th place is usually the same as finishing in 1,000th place—neither gets a prize. You are playing for **first place** (or the top few spots). 
- **Hold for the Royal:** In live play, you might keep a high pair instead of chasing a 3-card Royal Flush. In a tournament, you **always** go for the Royal.
- **Hold for Quads:** Bonus Poker Deluxe pays 80-for-1 on *any* Four of a Kind. If you have a choice between a 3-card Straight Flush and a pair, the pair is often better because it's a direct path to the high-scoring Quad.

### 3. Forget "Small Wins"
In a tournament, hitting Jacks or Better for 5 coins doesn't really matter. You need the **big hits**. 
- Don't worry about "saving" a hand. 
- Go for the big multipliers. 
- If you are trailing near the end of the tournament, you should take even more risks.

## Top Tactics for Winning

If you're entering a Bonus Poker Deluxe tournament, follow these four tactics to give yourself the best chance:

1.  **Practice for Speed:** Use a free Video Poker app and practice clicking "Deal" and "Draw" as fast as you can. Learn to recognize the optimal holds in a split second.
2.  **Focus on Quads:** Bonus Poker Deluxe is all about the 400-point Four of a Kind. Always hold pairs, even if it means breaking a potentially winning Straight or Flush.
3.  **Maximum Bet Always:** Most tournament machines are locked into the maximum bet, but if they aren't, **always bet the max**. You cannot win a tournament without the 4,000-point Royal Flush bonus.
4.  **Watch the Leaderboard:** If the tournament allows you to see the current leaders, keep an eye on them. If you are significantly behind and time is running out, you must go for "Hail Mary" plays (e.g., holding a single King or Ace to chase a Royal).

## Summary

Bonus Poker Deluxe tournaments are fast-paced and thrilling. By focusing on **speed** and **high-point hands** like Quads and Royals, you can overcome the luck of the draw and find yourself at the top of the leaderboard. Remember: it's not about the money; it's about the points. Good luck!
