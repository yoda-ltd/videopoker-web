## 3. How to Make the Switch (3 Simple Steps)

### Step 1: Learn the "Hierarchy"
In slots, you just match symbols. In All American, you are looking for specific poker hands. If you don't know poker hands, keep a small "Hand Hierarchy" chart next to you.
*   *Royal Flush > Straight Flush > 4 of a Kind > Full House > Flush > Straight > 3 of a Kind > Two Pair > High Pair.*

### Step 2: Use the "Hold" Strategy
This is the only new "skill" you need. Instead of just spinning, you choose which cards to keep. 
*   **Basic Rule for Slot Players:** If you see a card that is a Jack, Queen, King, or Ace, keep it! If you see two cards of the same rank (like two 7s), keep them!

### Step 3: Check the "Pay Table"
Slot players aren't used to looking at the math. In All American, the math is right there. Look for the numbers **8, 8, and 8** next to the Full House, Flush, and Straight. If you see those, you’ve found the best machine in the casino.
