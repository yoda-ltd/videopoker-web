Wild card games add excitement by designating one or more card ranks as wild — they can substitute for any card to complete a winning hand. Deuces Wild is the most popular, but this collection includes Sevens Wild, Threes Wild, and other rank-wild variants. Five of a Kind and Wild Royal Flush become possible, and the strategy shifts dramatically around counting and using wild cards.
