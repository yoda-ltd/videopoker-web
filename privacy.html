1. INTRODUCTION

Pure Video Poker ("we", "us", "our") operates the website at purevideopoker.com. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit and use our site. By accessing or using Pure Video Poker, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We are committed to minimal data collection. Pure Video Poker does not collect personal information from its users:

  • No account registration is required or offered.
  • No login credentials are collected or stored.
  • No payment information or financial data is collected.
  • No personal data such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers is requested.

Automatically collected information: When you visit our site, standard web server logs may record technical information including your IP address, browser type and version, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, and the date and time of your visit. This information is collected automatically by our hosting infrastructure and is used solely for site administration and security purposes.

3. COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

Our site uses cookies and similar tracking technologies for the following purposes:

  • Site Functionality: Essential cookies that enable core features such as remembering your game preferences and credit balance during a session.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site, including which pages are visited most frequently and how users navigate between pages. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous usage data.
  • Advertising: Google AdSense may place cookies on your device to serve advertisements. These cookies may be used to display personalized ads based on your browsing history across websites. Google uses the DoubleClick cookie and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect certain site functionality. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies, delete existing cookies, or alert you before a cookie is stored.

4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Analytics: Collects anonymous usage data to help us understand site traffic and user behavior. Google Analytics may use cookies and collects information such as pages visited, time on site, and general geographic location. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense: Serves advertisements on our site. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Personalization Settings.
  • Cloudflare: Provides content delivery network (CDN) services and security features for our site. Cloudflare may process certain technical data to deliver content efficiently and protect against threats. For more information, see Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Each of these third-party services operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their respective policies for detailed information about their data practices.

5. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

Any information collected through our site is used for the following purposes:

  • To improve site performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • To analyze traffic patterns and understand how visitors use our site.
  • To serve relevant advertisements through Google AdSense.
  • To maintain site security and prevent abuse.
  • To monitor and fix technical issues.

We do not sell, trade, or transfer any information to outside parties beyond the third-party services described above.

6. DATA RETENTION

Since Pure Video Poker does not collect or store personal data directly, there is no user data retained on our servers. Analytics data collected by Google Analytics is retained according to Google's standard data retention policies. Server logs maintained by our hosting provider are retained for a limited period for security and operational purposes.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Pure Video Poker is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information. If you believe a child under 13 may have provided information through our site, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Know: You have the right to understand what data is collected about you and how it is used.
  • Right to Opt Out: You can opt out of personalized advertising through Google's Ad Settings or the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.
  • Right to Disable Cookies: You can disable cookies at any time through your browser settings.
  • GDPR (European Users): If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation including the right to access, rectify, erase, and restrict the processing of your data. Since we do not collect personal data directly, these rights primarily apply to data collected by our third-party services.
  • CCPA (California Users): Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion of personal information, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

9. NO REAL MONEY GAMBLING

Pure Video Poker is provided strictly for entertainment and educational purposes only. This site does not offer real money gambling of any kind. There are no deposits, withdrawals, or financial transactions of any nature. All games use virtual play-money credits that have no monetary value. No financial information is collected or processed through this site.

10. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technologies, or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information. Your continued use of the site after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. CONTACT US

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or our data practices, please reach out to us through our site pages. We are committed to addressing your privacy concerns promptly and transparently.
