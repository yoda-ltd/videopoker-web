How to Play Jacks or Better Video Poker

Jacks or Better is the most popular video poker game in casinos. You win with a pair of Jacks, Queens, Kings, or Aces or better.

Rules

1. Choose BET 1 or BET 5 (always bet max for the 4,000 Royal Flush jackpot!)

2. Receive 5 cards. Click cards to HOLD them.

3. Press DRAW to replace unheld cards.

4. Win if your final hand matches the payout table above.
